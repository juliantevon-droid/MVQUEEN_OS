# 🗣️ MVQUEEN — Social Voice Guide

---

## Purpose

Define exactly how MVQUEEN speaks on every social platform — with real examples of right and wrong, platform-specific adaptations, and a voice test to apply before every post.

---

## The Social Voice Identity

MVQUEEN on social sounds like a woman who has arrived — quietly, confidently, without announcement. She doesn't perform for the algorithm. She creates for the woman she's speaking to.

**In one sentence:** Warm enough to feel human. Elevated enough to feel luxury. Real enough to feel true.

---

## Voice Dimensions on Social

| Dimension | Standard | Never |
|-----------|---------|-------|
| Intimacy | Speaks to one woman, not thousands | Broadcast announcements |
| Confidence | States things — doesn't hedge or over-explain | Self-deprecation, apologetic tone |
| Warmth | Feels like someone who knows you | Cold brand speak, corporate tone |
| Intelligence | Trusts her intelligence | Oversimplification, patronizing |
| Aspiration | Invites her into something better | Pressure, comparison, inadequacy |

## Instagram Voice

### Feed Posts
**Tone:** Aspirational, intimate, visually supported
**Length:** 80-150 words for narrative posts, under 50 for atmosphere posts
**Opening:** Must work as a standalone sentence — hooks before the "more" cut

**Right:**
*The ritual that changed my mornings wasn't complicated.*
*It was intentional.*
*[Product Name] — link in bio.*

**Wrong:**
*Hey girlies!! This product is literally SO AMAZING you guys need to try it ASAP!! Shop the link in bio before it sells out!! 🛍️🔥*

### Reels Captions
**Tone:** Conversational, real, slightly more casual than feed
**Length:** Under 80 words
**Opening:** Continues or expands the video hook

### Stories
**Tone:** Most casual format — behind the scenes, real moments, interactive
**Length:** Single thought per frame
**Voice:** Like texting a friend who has great taste

## TikTok Voice

TikTok demands realness. Less polish, more truth.

**Right:**
*Nobody talks about how skincare routines are supposed to restore you — not add to your to-do list. Here's what changed for me...*

**Wrong:**
*Hi everyone! Today I'm going to be showing you this amazing luxury skincare product from MVQUEEN that has completely transformed my skin!*

**Rules:**
- Speak naturally — no script-reading cadence
- Say the keyword out loud in the video (TikTok transcribes)
- End with a soft invite, never a hard sell
- Comment back on every comment in the first hour

---

## Pinterest Voice

Pinterest is search. Write for the woman who is actively looking.

**Right:**
*Luxury face serum for women who want their morning ritual to feel intentional. Lightweight, absorbs instantly, stays soft all day. Part of the MVQUEEN ritual.*

**Wrong:**
*OMG this serum is everything!! So obsessed with how my skin looks!*

**Rules:**
- First sentence contains primary keyword
- Descriptive and atmospheric
- 100-150 words for maximum reach
- Hashtags at end: 3-5 relevant, not excessive

---

## Facebook Voice

Facebook skews slightly older, more conversational, community-first.

**Right:**
*For the women in this community — what does your evening ritual look like? We're always curious what the women who find MVQUEEN are already doing before they find us.*

**Wrong:**
*SHOP NOW! New arrivals just dropped and they're going fast!!*

**Rules:**
- Community questions drive engagement better than announcements
- Longer captions acceptable — Facebook reads them
- Repurpose Instagram content but adapt the opening line

## Voice Test (Apply Before Every Post)

```
□ Does this sound like MVQUEEN — or like any other brand?
□ Does it speak to one woman — not an audience?
□ Is the first line strong enough to stand alone?
□ Is every word earning its place?
□ Does it make her feel elevated — not pressured?
□ Would she save or share this?
□ Does it pass the forbidden words check?
```

If any box is unchecked — rewrite before posting.
