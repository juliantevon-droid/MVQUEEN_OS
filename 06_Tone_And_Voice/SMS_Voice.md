# 📱 MVQUEEN — SMS Voice Guide

---

## SMS Is Different

SMS is the most direct channel MVQUEEN has.
No algorithm. No competition for attention. It lands on her lock screen.

That means two things:
1. It must earn its place — never feel spammy or frivolous
2. It must be immediately valuable, beautiful, or both

MVQUEEN SMS is short, warm, specific, and personal.
It sounds like a message from a brand she loves — not a brand that bought her number.

---

## SMS Voice Rules

**Rule 1 — Under 160 characters when possible**
She reads it in one glance. Make that glance count.

**Rule 2 — Lead with the value**
She sees the first 40 characters before she decides to open. Start with what matters.

**Rule 3 — One message, one action**
One reason to text. One link. One thing she can do.

**Rule 4 — Warm but efficient**
Not cold. Not robotic. But also not padded. Say it in her language, then stop.

**Rule 5 — Never pressure**
No countdown clocks in SMS. No "HURRY." No "LAST CHANCE" in all caps.
Urgency through tone, never through panic.

## What MVQUEEN Never Sends via SMS

- Mass blasts with no personalization signal
- More than 4 SMS per month (unless VIP tier with explicit preference)
- Urgency language in all caps
- Multiple links
- Anything that could have been an email — SMS is reserved for high-value, time-sensitive moments
- Generic promotional language ("Sale ends soon!!!")
