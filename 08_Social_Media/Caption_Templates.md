# 👑 MVQUEEN — Caption Templates
### Ready-to-Use Frameworks for Every Platform

---

## What This File Is

Caption templates are not fill-in-the-blank forms. They are structural frameworks — proven emotional patterns that MVQUEEN copy follows — with clear guidance on how to adapt them to any product, pillar, or platform.

Every template here is built on the Hook Systems (Hook_Systems.md) and Writing Rules (Writing_Rules.md). The structure is the vehicle. The MVQUEEN voice is what fills it.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS FILE

1. Identify which **Content Pillar** your post belongs to
2. Choose the template that fits the emotional purpose
3. Fill in the [brackets] with specific, brand-voice copy
4. Run it through the Voice Consistency Rules checklist
5. Remove the brackets — the caption should read seamlessly

Never publish a template uncustomized. The brackets are placeholders, not the product.

## SECTION 1 — INSTAGRAM CAPTION TEMPLATES

### Template 01 — The Scene Open (Pillar: Feminine Atmosphere)
*Creates atmosphere before the product is named.*

```
[Sensory scene — one sentence. Drop her into the feeling.]

[Second sentence that deepens it. What she notices next.]

[The product or brand truth — quiet, not shouted.]

[Optional: One-line identity close.]

Shop via link in bio.
```

**Example:**
> The morning light came in at exactly the right angle. Everything on the shelf seemed to glow a little.
>
> Some mornings just cooperate.
>
> The Glow Serum. Part of the ritual that earns mornings like this.
>
> *For women who treat their skincare like it matters.*
>
> Shop via link in bio.

*Structured, scannable, personality-led.*

```

→ [Item 1 — sensory or emotional]
→ [Item 2]
→ [Item 3]
→ [Item 4 — optional MVQUEEN product naturally placed]

[Closing line — identity or atmosphere.]

[CTA if product is featured.]
```

**Example:**

>

> → Coffee before the phone
> → The light coming in slow
> → The serum that actually works

### Template 07 — The Micro Essay (Pillar: Emotional Femininity / Brand World)
*Long-form caption for deep engagement. Instagram allows up to 2,200 characters.*

```
[Hook — one line. Stops the scroll.]

[Body — 3–5 short paragraphs. Tell the story. Make the point. Be specific.]

[Landing line — the one sentence that stays with her.]

[Optional CTA — gentle, not forced.]
```

**Example:**
> She stopped waiting for the right occasion.
>
> There's a version of self-care that waits for permission. For the special day, the accomplishment, the reward that justifies the indulgence. And then there's the other kind — the kind that decides Tuesday counts, that the candle gets lit on a regular evening, that the serum gets used because the morning deserves it, not because something extraordinary happened.
>
> MVQUEEN was built for the second kind. For the woman who decided her ordinary life was worth elevating — not someday, but now.
>
> Because luxury was never about the occasion. It was always about the decision.
>
> The collection is open. It's been waiting for you.

## SECTION 3 — PINTEREST CAPTION TEMPLATES

Pinterest captions are descriptions built for search AND desire. They work as standalone copy the reader never sees — but that gets the pin found.

### Pinterest Template 01 — The Guide Pin
```
[What she'll learn or get from this pin — keyword-forward]
[2–3 sentences of educational or aspirational value]
[Brand name + collection reference]
```

**Example:**
> How to build a morning skincare ritual that actually changes how your day starts. The right order, the right products, the right intention — this guide covers all three. Discover the MVQUEEN skincare collection at the link.

---

### Pinterest Template 02 — The Aspiration Pin
```
[The world this pin lives in — one sentence]
[What she'll feel if she saves and lives this]
[Product or collection reference]
```

**Example:**
> The feminine morning aesthetic that makes waking up feel like a reward. Soft skincare rituals, warm light, and products that make the routine feel like the point — not the preparation. MVQUEEN Beauty Collection.
