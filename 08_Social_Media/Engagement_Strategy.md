# 💬 MVQUEEN — Engagement Strategy

---

## Purpose

Build a genuine, loyal community through intentional engagement — turning passive followers into active advocates who feel connected to the MVQUEEN world.

Engagement is not a vanity metric. It is relationship infrastructure.
The algorithm rewards it. More importantly — women remember it.

---

## Engagement Philosophy

MVQUEEN engages like a woman who is present — not a brand managing reputation.

Every comment, DM, and reply is an opportunity to make one woman feel genuinely seen.
That feeling compounds into loyalty, advocacy, and lifetime value.

**The standard:** Every engagement response sounds human.
If it could have been written by a bot — rewrite it.

---

## Daily Engagement Ritual (30 minutes)

```
□ Reply to all comments on posts from last 24 hours (10 min)
□ Respond to DMs and story replies (10 min)
□ Engage with followers' content — like, comment genuinely (5 min)
□ Engage with niche hashtag community posts (5 min)
```

**Comment reply standard:**
- Always personalize — never copy-paste
- Add value or warmth — never just an emoji
- Ask a follow-up question when natural
- Response time target: Under 2 hours on launch days, under 4 hours standard

---

## Comment Response Framework

### Positive comment
Acknowledge specifically + add warmth + optional question.
*"This means everything — especially coming from you. What's your ritual like in the morning? 🤍"*

### Question comment
Answer specifically + invite further conversation.
*"Great question! [Specific answer]. Let me know if you want more detail on [topic]!"*

### Negative comment (public)
Acknowledge + invite to DM + never be defensive.
*"We hear you and we're sorry this wasn't the experience you deserved. Please DM us — we want to make this right personally."*

### UGC / tag
Celebrate + repost to stories + personal reply.
*"This is exactly the energy we built MVQUEEN for. You look incredible. 🤍"*

---

## Community Growth Through Engagement

### Proactive Engagement (Go to them)
Spend 15 minutes daily engaging with:
- Posts tagged with branded hashtags
- Posts in niche community hashtags
- Followers' own content
- Comments on competitor posts (without mentioning MVQUEEN)

**Comment quality standard:**
Leave comments that add value — observations, genuine compliments, real questions.
Never: "Nice post!" / "Check out our page!" / generic emoji spam

### Reactive Engagement (Respond to them)
- Reply to every comment within 4 hours
- Reply to every DM within 4 hours (business hours)
- Reply to every story reply within 8 hours
- Like every tagged post within 24 hours
