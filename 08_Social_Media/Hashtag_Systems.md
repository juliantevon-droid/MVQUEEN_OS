# #️⃣ MVQUEEN — Hashtag Systems

---

## Purpose

Build a strategic, organized hashtag system that maximizes content discovery, grows the right audience, and reinforces brand positioning across every platform.

Hashtags are a discovery tool — not decoration.
Every hashtag set should be intentional, tested, and updated regularly.

---

## Hashtag Strategy by Platform

### Instagram

**Set structure (per post):**
- 1-2 branded hashtags
- 3-4 niche community hashtags
- 3-4 content-specific hashtags
- 1-2 broad discovery hashtags
- Total: 8-12 per post

**Size strategy:**
Mix hashtag sizes for maximum reach:
| Size | Follower Range | Purpose |
|------|--------------|---------|
| Micro | Under 100K posts | High chance of appearing in top posts |
| Mid | 100K-1M posts | Balanced reach and competition |
| Macro | 1M-10M posts | Discovery by volume |
| Brand | Any size | Community building |
