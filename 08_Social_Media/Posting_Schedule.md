# 📅 MVQUEEN — Posting Schedule

---

## Purpose

Define the optimal posting cadence for every platform — ensuring consistent brand presence without burning out content resources or diluting quality with volume.

Consistency is a luxury signal. Sporadic posting signals instability.
But volume without quality is worse than quality without volume.

## Posting Cadence by Platform

### Instagram
| Format | Frequency | Best Days | Best Times |
|--------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Feed posts | 4-5x/week | Tue, Wed, Thu, Sat | 9-11am or 6-8pm EST |
| Reels | 3-4x/week | Mon, Wed, Fri | 9am-12pm EST |
| Stories | Daily (5-10 frames) | Every day | Morning + evening |
| Lives | 1-2x/month | Thursday evening | 7-9pm EST |

### TikTok
| Format | Frequency | Best Days | Best Times |
|--------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Videos | 1-2x/day | Tue-Fri | 7-9am, 12-3pm, 7-9pm EST |
| Minimum viable | 5x/week | — | — |

*TikTok rewards consistency and volume more than any other platform.*

### Pinterest
| Format | Frequency | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Fresh pins | 5-10x/day | Use scheduler — Pinterest rewards volume |
| Idea pins | 3-5x/week | Higher reach format |
| Board maintenance | Weekly | Add to boards, refresh descriptions |

*Pinterest is a search engine — evergreen content compounds over time.*

### Email
| Type | Frequency | Best Days | Best Times |
|------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Campaign emails | 2-3x/week | Tue, Wed, Thu | 10am-12pm or 7-9pm EST |
| Maximum | 3x/week | — | Never more than 1/day |
| Minimum | 1x/week | — | Consistency over frequency |

### Facebook
| Format | Frequency | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Feed posts | 3-4x/week | Repurpose from Instagram |
| Stories | 3-5x/week | Repurpose from Instagram |

### YouTube
| Format | Frequency | Best Days |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Long-form videos | 1-2x/month | Wednesday or Thursday |
| YouTube Shorts | 3-5x/week | Repurpose from TikTok |

## Weekly Content Rhythm

| Day | Priority Tasks |
|-----|---------------|
| Monday | Plan week's content, generate AI drafts, review and approve |
| Tuesday | Film/photograph content if needed, schedule week's posts |
| Wednesday | Publish priority content, engage with comments |
| Thursday | Email send day (primary campaign), Pinterest batch upload |
| Friday | Analyze week's performance, capture top performers |
| Saturday | Lifestyle/community content, lighter engagement |
| Sunday | Plan next week, review content calendar |

## Content Batching System

Batch content production to protect time:

**Weekly batch (2-3 hours):**
- Generate all social captions for the week (AI-assisted)
- Review and edit all captions
- Schedule all posts

**Bi-weekly batch (3-4 hours):**
- Film 6-10 TikTok/Reel videos
- Edit and caption
- Schedule in advance

**Monthly batch (4-6 hours):**
- Pinterest pin creation (40-60 pins)
- Email campaign drafts for the month
- Blog article drafts
