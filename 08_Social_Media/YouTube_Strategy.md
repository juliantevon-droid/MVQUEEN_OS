# 📺 MVQUEEN — YouTube Strategy

---

## Purpose

Build MVQUEEN's YouTube presence as a long-form brand authority channel — creating content that earns deep trust, demonstrates genuine expertise, and drives qualified traffic to the store over time.

YouTube is the long game. Plant seeds now that compound for years.

---

## YouTube Strategic Position

MVQUEEN on YouTube is:
- The thoughtful expert in feminine beauty and lifestyle
- The brand that goes deeper than other channels allow
- The destination for women who want more than a product review

MVQUEEN on YouTube is NOT:
- A product haul channel
- A trend-chasing content farm
- A hard-sell environment

---

## Content Pillars for YouTube

### Pillar 1 — Ritual Deep Dives
Long-form guides to building intentional beauty and lifestyle rituals.

**Examples:**
- *"How I Built a Morning Ritual That Actually Works (Not a 5am Routine)"*
- *"The Evening Ritual That Changed How I Sleep and How My Skin Looks"*
- *"Building a Minimalist Skincare Routine for Real Women"*

**Length:** 10-20 minutes
**Search intent:** Informational — women building or improving their routines

---

### Pillar 2 — Ingredient and Product Education
In-depth education on skincare ingredients, hair science, and fashion quality.

**Examples:**
- *"Skincare Ingredients Explained: What Actually Works and What's Hype"*
- *"How to Read a Skincare Label (Without a Chemistry Degree)"*
- *"What 'Luxury Fabric' Actually Means — and How to Identify It"*

**Length:** 8-15 minutes
**Search intent:** Educational — women who want to make informed decisions

---

### Pillar 3 — Brand Philosophy Content
Long-form explorations of MVQUEEN's values and feminine philosophy.

**Examples:**
- *"Why I Built MVQUEEN: The Brand Philosophy Behind the Products"*
- *"Softness Is Strength: A Different Way to Think About Femininity"*
- *"The Problem With Modern Beauty Culture (And What We're Doing About It)"*

**Length:** 10-25 minutes
**Search intent:** Brand/identity — women who want to connect with something deeper

---

### Pillar 4 — Product Showcases and Honest Reviews
In-depth, honest coverage of MVQUEEN products — what they do, who they're for, real results.

**Examples:**
- *"[Product Name] — 30 Day Honest Review"*
- *"Testing Our Entire Skincare Range on Real Skin"*
- *"The MVQUEEN Ritual Kit: A Complete Walkthrough"*

**Length:** 8-15 minutes
**Search intent:** Commercial — women considering a purchase

---

## YouTube SEO Standards

Every video is optimized for search:

| Element | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| Title | Primary keyword first, brand voice, under 60 characters |
| Description | 200+ words, keyword-rich, links to products and related content |
| Tags | 10-15 relevant tags, mix of specific and broad |
| Thumbnail | Readable at small size, consistent brand aesthetic, curiosity-gap text |
| Chapters | Time-stamped chapters for videos over 8 minutes |
| End screen | Subscribe + 2 recommended videos |
| Cards | Link to relevant products or videos at natural moments |

---

## Publishing Cadence

**Starting cadence:** 1 video per month (quality over volume)
**Growth cadence:** 2 videos per month when production process is stable
**YouTube Shorts:** 3-5 per week repurposed from TikTok content
