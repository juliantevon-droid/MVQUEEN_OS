## Purpose

Curate and analyze the boutique and luxury brand experiences that inform MVQUEEN's aesthetic, customer experience design, and brand positioning.

What the best boutique brands do — we study, filter, and apply.

---

## Boutique Brand Analysis Framework

When studying any boutique or luxury brand for inspiration:

| Element | What to Note |
|---------|------------|
| Visual identity | Color, typography, photography style, packaging |
| Store experience | Layout, lighting, scent, music, staff approach |
| Digital experience | Website flow, product page design, checkout |
| Voice and tone | How they write, what they never say |
| Pricing psychology | How they frame price and value |
| Customer service | How they handle issues and build loyalty |
| Community | How they build belonging around the brand |
| Content | What they create and what performs |
