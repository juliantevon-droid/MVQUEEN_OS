HOW PERSONAS WORK
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Each persona controls:
- tone: the writing voice and register
- emotional_axes: the emotional dimensions emphasized
- seo_bias: keyword strategy direction
- cta_style: call-to-action approach


The engine detects product category → selects best-fit persona → generates all copy through that persona's lens.
