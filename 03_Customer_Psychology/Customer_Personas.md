# 👑 MVQUEEN CUSTOMER PERSONAS  
*The complete identity‑based, emotional, aesthetic, and behavioral personas that represent MVQUEEN’s core customer archetypes*

---

# 1. Purpose of the Customer Personas System

Customer Personas define **who MVQUEEN serves**, not by demographics, but by:

- emotional identity  
- aesthetic world  
- behavioral patterns  
- luxury perception  
- feminine expression  
- lifestyle aspirations  
- psychological drivers  

These personas guide:

- product development  
- marketing strategy  
- brand messaging  
- visual identity  
- customer experience  
- AI agent personalization  

Personas are not stereotypes.  
They are **emotional identities**.

---

# 2. MVQUEEN’s Persona Philosophy

MVQUEEN personas are built on four pillars:

### **1. Emotional Identity**
How she feels and wants to feel.

### **2. Aesthetic Identity**
Her visual world and beauty preferences.

### **3. Behavioral Identity**
How she shops, decides, and engages.

### **4. Aspirational Identity**
Who she is becoming.
