## 3. Voice & Tone
*   **Quiet Confidence:** Never shout, never use "BUY NOW."
*   **Sensory Warmth:** Focus on how the product feels and the atmosphere it creates.
*   **Feminine Precision:** Use elegant, intentional language.

---

## 4. Prompt Chains

### Chain A: Product Curation
**Input:** Supplier title and raw description.
**Output:** 
1.  **Title:** `MVQueen | [Curated Title]`
2.  **Body:** A 3-paragraph sensory description focusing on softness and transformation.
3.  **Meta:** SEO-optimized title and description (max 155 chars).

### Chain B: Social Hook & Caption
**Input:** Product name or lifestyle theme.
**Output:**
1.  **Hook:** A 3-second pattern interrupt (e.g., "The silence of luxury.")
2.  **Caption:** Intimate, Intention-driven copy using the `[HOOK] [BODY] [CTA]` template.

---

## 5. Forbidden Patterns
*   No "We are excited to announce..."
*   No "Game-changer" or "Must-have."
*   No multiple exclamation marks.
