# 🔗 MVQUEEN — Cross-Sell Systems

---

## Purpose

Define the strategic cross-sell architecture for MVQUEEN — ensuring every product recommendation feels like a personal suggestion from someone who knows her, not an automated upsell attempt.

---

## Cross-Sell Philosophy

The best cross-sell at MVQUEEN answers the question she hasn't asked yet:
*"What else would complete this ritual?"*

Not: *"Other people also bought..."*
But: *"She was made to be paired with..."*

---

## Cross-Sell Logic Framework

### Ritual Completion
Recommend products that complete a ritual she has started.

| Primary Product | Ritual Completion Cross-Sell |
|----------------|------------------------------|
| Cleanser | Toner, serum, moisturizer |
| Serum | Moisturizer, SPF, eye cream |
| Hair mask | Leave-in conditioner, hair oil |
| Body wash | Body lotion, body oil |
| Dress | Belt, jewelry, complementary piece |

### Category Expansion
Introduce her to a new category through what she already loves.

| Primary Category | Expansion Cross-Sell |
|-----------------|---------------------|
| Skincare buyer | Introduce haircare |
| Haircare buyer | Introduce skincare |
| Fashion buyer | Introduce accessories |
| Accessories buyer | Introduce fragrance |

### Sensory Pairing
Products that share a scent, texture, or mood.

*"If she loves [scent A], she'll find [product B] feels like the same world."*

---

## Cross-Sell Placement Strategy

### On Product Pages
**Section title:** *"She pairs beautifully with..."*
**Max recommendations:** 3
**Selection criteria:** Genuine ritual connection — not just bestsellers

### In Cart
**Section title:** *"Complete the ritual"*
**Max recommendations:** 1-2
**Selection criteria:** Most natural next step in her routine

### In Post-Purchase Email (Day 14)
**Framing:** *"You've had [product] for two weeks — here's what she loves beside her"*
**Max recommendations:** 1
**Timing:** Only after she's had time to fall in love with the first purchase

### In Loyalty Email (VIP Segment)
**Framing:** *"Because you know your ritual — here's what's waiting to join it"*
**Selection:** Based on her actual purchase history

---

## Cross-Sell Copy Standards

**Always frame as:**
- A ritual recommendation
- Something that "belongs with" what she has
- A natural next step in her journey

**Never frame as:**
- "Frequently bought together" (Amazon language)
- "You might also want" (transactional)
- "Don't forget to add" (pressuring)
- Discount-driven pairing ("Buy both, save X%")
