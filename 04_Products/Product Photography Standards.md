# Photography Direction

Photography should feel:
- emotionally immersive
- intimate
- feminine
- atmospheric
- cinematic
- naturally elevated
- emotionally expressive
- visually calm

The photography should avoid:
- emotionally empty perfection
- hyper-commercial posing
- trend-overload
- excessive editing
- emotionally disconnected glamour
- visual aggression

The imagery should feel emotionally alive rather than commercially manufactured.
