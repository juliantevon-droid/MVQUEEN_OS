## Hair Category Philosophy

MVQUEEN haircare is built on one conviction:
**Healthy hair is the foundation. Everything else follows.**

The woman who shops MVQUEEN hair products is not chasing a trend.
She is building a practice — learning what her hair actually needs and giving it that.

MVQUEEN haircare serves:
- The woman rebuilding hair health after damage or neglect
- The woman simplifying her routine to fewer, better products
- The woman who wants her haircare to feel like a ritual — not a chore
- The woman who values scalp health as the beginning of everything

## Formulation Philosophy

MVQUEEN hair products prioritize:

**Scalp health first** — a healthy scalp grows healthy hair. Products that address the root (literally) before the length.

**Moisture over protein balance** — most women's hair needs more moisture than protein. Formulations lean toward hydration.

**Ingredient integrity** — no ingredients that create short-term aesthetic results at the cost of long-term hair health.

**Fragrance restraint** — light, clean scents that don't overwhelm. Fragrance is never the primary feature.

---

## Hair Product Sourcing Standards

```
□ Ingredient list reviewed — no known harmful ingredients
□ Tested on real hair types representative of target audience
□ pH appropriate for hair and scalp
□ Performance tested: Does it deliver what it claims?
□ Texture and application experience evaluated
□ Scent assessed — appropriate for brand
□ Packaging appropriate for product viscosity and use
□ Shelf life and stability confirmed
□ Sample order before full inventory commitment
```

## Hair Photography Standards

Hair product photography requires:
- Before and after content (ethical — showing real, achievable results)
- In-use application shots
- Texture shots of the product itself
- Lifestyle shots — her in her ritual, product in her hand
- Diverse representation — different hair textures, lengths, and types

---

## Hair Copy Standards

Hair product copy at MVQUEEN:
- Leads with how hair feels after — not ingredient lists
- Addresses the specific hair type or concern directly
- Names the ritual context — when and how in her routine
- Honest about results — no overclaiming

**Wrong:** *"Transform your hair with our revolutionary formula!"*
**Right:** *"From rough and tired to soft and alive — the weekly ritual her hair has been asking for."*
