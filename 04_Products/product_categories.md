# 2. MVQUEEN’s Category Philosophy

MVQUEEN organizes products using four pillars:

### **1. Emotional Purpose**
How the product makes her feel.

### **2. Identity Expression**
How the product reflects who she is or who she is becoming.

### **3. Aesthetic Alignment**
How the product fits into her soft glam world.

### **4. Functional Role**
How the product supports her beauty, lifestyle, or rituals.

Categories must reflect **emotion + identity + aesthetic + function**.
