## Ad Copy System Context

*Load this in addition to the Master System Context for all ad copy generation:*

```
AD COPY ADDITIONAL CONTEXT:

Ad copy at MVQUEEN serves one of three objectives:
1. AWARENESS — Introduce MVQUEEN to a new audience. Focus: identity and world-building.
2. CONVERSION — Drive purchase from warm or retargeting audiences. Focus: specific product desire.
3. RETENTION — Re-engage lapsed customers. Focus: warmth and belonging, not guilt.

The most important rule in MVQUEEN ad copy:
Never manufacture anxiety. Never exploit insecurity. Never use artificial urgency.
Create desire through resonance — make her feel seen, not pressured.

Ad copy elements:
- Primary text: 2-3 sentences max. Lead with identity or feeling. Never feature-first.
- Headline: 4-8 words. Punchy. Works as a standalone statement.
- Description: 1 line. Supports headline. Optional.
- CTA button: From CTA Library — not generic "Shop Now" where possible.
```

## Ad Copy Testing Protocol

For every ad campaign:

```
LAUNCH WITH:
- Minimum 2 creative variations per ad set
- Each variation tests a different angle (identity vs. sensory vs. social proof)
- Same target audience for valid comparison

KILL RULE:
- Under 0.5% CTR after 1,000 impressions → pause and replace

SCALE RULE:
- ROAS 3x+ for 7 days → increase budget 20%
- Top performer identified → create 2 more variations on same angle

LOG:
- Every test result in 37_Databases/Campaign_Log.md
- Note: angle, audience, creative type, CTR, ROAS, decision
```
