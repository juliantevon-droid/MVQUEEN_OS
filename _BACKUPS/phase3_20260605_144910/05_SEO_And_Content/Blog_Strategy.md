# ✍️ MVQUEEN — Blog Strategy

---

## Purpose

Build MVQUEEN's blog as a long-term SEO and brand authority asset — attracting qualified organic traffic, demonstrating genuine expertise, and creating content that earns trust before the sale.

---

## Blog Strategic Position

The MVQUEEN blog is:
- An editorial publication for women who care about beauty, lifestyle, and intentional living
- An SEO asset that drives qualified traffic to the store
- A brand authority builder that demonstrates expertise
- A trust-building tool that converts readers into customers over time

The MVQUEEN blog is NOT:
- A product catalogue dressed as content
- A keyword-stuffed SEO farm
- Generic beauty content that any brand could publish

---

## Content Pillars for Blog

### Pillar 1 — Ritual & Routine
**Goal:** SEO traffic + brand alignment
**Topics:** Morning rituals, evening rituals, weekly self-care, building intentional routines
**Search intent:** Informational — women building or improving routines
**Frequency:** 2x per month

### Pillar 2 — Ingredient & Product Education
**Goal:** SEO traffic + purchase confidence
**Topics:** Skincare ingredients, hair care science, fashion quality indicators
**Search intent:** Educational — women researching before buying
**Frequency:** 2x per month

### Pillar 3 — Lifestyle & Philosophy
**Goal:** Brand depth + community building
**Topics:** Feminine living, soft life philosophy, intentional consumption
**Search intent:** Inspirational — women seeking identity alignment
**Frequency:** 1x per month

### Pillar 4 — Product Features
**Goal:** Commercial — drive purchase
**Topics:** In-depth product guides, comparisons, ritual integrations
**Search intent:** Commercial — women considering specific products
**Frequency:** 1x per month

---

## Article Structure Standards

Every MVQUEEN blog article follows this arc:

```
HEADLINE: Keyword-led, brand voice, under 65 characters
OPEN: Her world — a moment she recognizes (never a definition or statistic)
BODY: Value delivery in MVQUEEN voice — specific, warm, expert
BRAND BRIDGE: One paragraph connecting value back to MVQUEEN philosophy
CLOSE: Invitation — not just a conclusion
CTA: Relevant product or next article
```

---

## SEO Standards for Blog

| Element | Standard |
|---------|----------|
| Word count | 1,200-2,500 words |
| Primary keyword | In H1, first 100 words, 2-3 H2s |
| Secondary keywords | Naturally distributed throughout |
| Internal links | 2-3 per article minimum |
| Meta title | 50-60 characters |
| Meta description | 150-160 characters |
| Images | 2-4 per article, all with alt text |

---

## Publishing Cadence

**Starting:** 2-4 articles per month
**Growth:** 6-8 articles per month when content team in place
**AI-assisted:** Generate drafts using Blog Prompts, human edit and publish
