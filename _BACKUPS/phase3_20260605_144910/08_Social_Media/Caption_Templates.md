# 📝 MVQUEEN — Caption Templates

---

## Purpose

A library of reusable caption frameworks and fill-in templates — for fast, on-brand content production across all platforms.

Templates are starting points — always personalize before posting.

---

## Instagram Caption Templates

### Template C-01 — Product Feature
```
[Sensory opening line — how it feels or what it does]
[Second line — the transformation]

[Product Name] — [brief brand description]
[CTA — link in bio / shop at the link]

[2-3 hashtags]
```

**Example:**
*She melts in on contact. By morning, the kind of softness you notice first.*
*[Product Name] — the serum your ritual has been missing.*
*Shop at the link in bio.*
`#MVQUEEN #skincareritual #luxuryskincare`

---

### Template C-02 — Lifestyle/Atmosphere
```
[3 short lines creating atmosphere — sensory, slow, intentional]
[Brand connection — 1 line]
[Optional soft CTA]
```

**Example:**
*Soft lighting. Slower morning. Nothing to prove.*
*Beauty that belongs to you.*
*[Link in bio]*

---

### Template C-03 — Identity Statement
```
[Declaration — 1-2 lines that name who she is]
[Brand connection — how MVQUEEN was made for her]
[CTA — optional]
```

**Example:**
*For the woman who is done performing and ready to just live.*
*MVQUEEN was built for her.*

---

### Template C-04 — Educational
```
[Hook — question or revelation]
[2-4 lines of value — specific and useful]
[Brand connection — 1 line]
[Save prompt]
```

**Example:**
*The difference between a routine and a ritual:*
*A routine is something you do. A ritual is something you feel.*
*One drains. The other restores.*
*MVQUEEN was built for the ritual.*
*Save this.*

---

### Template C-05 — Community/UGC Feature
```
[@handle] [specific celebration of what they shared]
[Brand connection — 1 line]
[Invitation for others]
```

**Example:**
*@softskinlife showed us what a MVQUEEN morning looks like — and we can't stop looking.*
*This is exactly the ritual we built this for.*
*Show us yours. Tag @mvqueen. #MvQueenLife*
