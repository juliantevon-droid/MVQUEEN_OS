## What Makes Content Go Viral

Content spreads when it creates one of these reactions:
1. **Recognition** — *"That's exactly me."*
2. **Revelation** — *"I never thought of it that way."*
3. **Resonance** — *"She said what I couldn't say."*
4. **Utility** — *"I need to save this."*
5. **Beauty** — *"I need to share this."*

Every MVQUEEN viral hook triggers at least one of these.

## Viral Hook Formulas by Platform

### TikTok / Reels Hooks (First 2 seconds)

**Formula V1 — The Painful Truth**
*"Nobody tells you that [uncomfortable truth about beauty/self-care/femininity]"*

Examples:
- *"Nobody tells you that most skincare routines are designed to make you dependent, not healed."*
- *"Nobody tells you that 'treat yourself' culture was designed to make you feel guilty until you spend."*
- *"Nobody tells you that your morning routine is supposed to restore you, not exhaust you."*

---

**Formula V2 — The Hot Take**
*"[Controversial but true opinion about beauty/luxury/femininity]"*

Examples:
- *"Expensive skincare isn't the problem. Not knowing what you actually need is."*
- *"The 'soft life' trend missed the point entirely."*
- *"Most luxury brands don't actually like their customers."*

---

**Formula V3 — The Specific Permission**
*"If you [very specific scenario she recognizes] — this is for you."*

Examples:
- *"If you've been buying skincare for a year and still don't love your skin — this is for you."*
- *"If your morning routine feels like a chore instead of a ritual — this is for you."*
- *"If you've been waiting for a brand that treats you like you have taste — this is for you."*

---

**Formula V4 — The Story Open**
*"I used to [relatable struggle] until [turning point]."*

Examples:
- *"I used to spend $400 on skincare and still feel nothing until I realized what I was actually missing."*
- *"I used to think luxury wasn't for women like me until I understood what luxury actually means."*

---

**Formula V5 — The Comparison Flip**
*"[Expected outcome] vs [unexpected better outcome]"*

Examples:
- *"Expensive routine vs intentional routine — here's what actually changes your skin."*
- *"Beauty as performance vs beauty as restoration — the difference is everything."*

## Viral Content Formats

### Format 1 — The Revelation List
*"X things I wish someone told me about [topic]"*
- 3-7 items
- Each one specific and actionable
- Last item is most powerful — save the best for the end

### Format 2 — The Before/After (Not Physical)
Show the emotional or lifestyle transformation — not physical appearance.
*"My beauty routine before MVQUEEN / after MVQUEEN"*
Focus: How she feels, how mornings feel, her relationship with beauty

### Format 3 — The POV
*"POV: You finally found a brand that was built for you"*
Immersive, second-person, atmospheric

### Format 4 — The Aesthetic Day-in-the-Life
No voiceover. Just visuals + text overlay.
Her morning. Her ritual. Her products. Her softness.
ASMR-adjacent — slow, intentional, beautiful.

### Format 5 — The Educational Series
5-part series on one topic (skincare ingredients, hair health, etc.)
Each part ends with a hook to the next.
Drives follows and saves.

## Viral Hook Testing Protocol

For every 5 pieces of content — test at least 2 different hooks on the same content.

**Track:**
- Views in first hour (TikTok signal)
- Watch time / completion rate
- Shares and saves
- Comments (are they engaging with the topic?)
