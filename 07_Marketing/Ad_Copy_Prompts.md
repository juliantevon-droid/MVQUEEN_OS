# 👑 MVQUEEN — Ad Copy Prompts
### Paid Advertising Copy System

The fundamental principle: the ad should feel like content first, conversion vehicle second. She stops because it resonates. She clicks because she wants what's behind it.

---

## THE AD COPY PHILOSOPHY
Most ads announce. MVQUEEN ads invite.
❌ "Shop MVQUEEN now. 30% off — limited time!"
✅ "The serum I've worn every morning for 4 months. Finally back in stock."

## PART 1 — META / FACEBOOK / INSTAGRAM ADS

### System Prompt
```
SYSTEM: Writing Meta ad copy for MVQUEEN.
Voice: Warm, confident, specific. Lead with feeling before product. No panic urgency. No discount language unless a sale is real.

Components:
- Primary text (first 125 chars most important): Hook + 1–2 lines
- Headline (40 chars max): specific, benefit-led, not "Shop MVQUEEN"
- Description (30 chars): reinforce or add context

Three variations required: Emotional / Benefit-led / Social proof
Forbidden: countdown, "LIMITED TIME!!!", all-caps, vague luxury claims, generic CTA alone
CTA buttons: "Shop Now" / "Learn More" / "Get Offer" — earn the click before it appears
```

## PART 2 — TIKTOK ADS

### System Prompt
```
SYSTEM: Writing TikTok ad scripts for MVQUEEN.
- First 2–3 seconds: organic content feel — NOT an ad
- No branded intro in first 3 seconds
- Voice: first-person, real, like a genuine recommendation
- Length: 15–30s conversion | up to 60s story
- CTA: "Link in bio" or "Shop the link" — never countdown

Format:
[0–3s]: Visual hook + spoken hook
[3–20s]: Demo/story/benefit
[20–30s]: Natural CTA
```

### Scripts

**Skincare — 15s:**
[0–3s] Close-up serum on fingertips, warm light. Text: "The step I used to skip."
[3–12s] (VO) I used to rush through my morning. Then I added this and everything slowed down. The Glow Serum — 60 seconds, works all day.
[12–15s] My skin has looked like this for 4 months. Shop the link.

**Fragrance — 30s:**
[0–3s] Slow fragrance spray, warm light. No text. Soft music.
[3–20s] (VO) I've been asked what I'm wearing every week for two months. Warm floral that opens soft and settles into something that feels like it was made for your skin. 8-hour wear. Refillable.
[20–30s] If you've been looking for your signature scent — worth trying. Link in bio.

**Fashion — 30s:**
[0–3s] Getting dressed, morning light. Text: "The outfit that changed how I walk into rooms."
[3–22s] (VO) I don't get dressed for other people anymore. I get dressed for the version of me that walks in knowing she's already handled everything. The Ivory Slip — bias cut, fluid, wears with everything. Worn it 12 times since it arrived.
[22–30s] Link in bio. Free shipping over $65.

```
SYSTEM: Writing Pinterest promoted pins for MVQUEEN.
Components:
```

## AD COPY DO / DON'T

✅ DO: Lead with feeling or specific detail | First-person voice in social | Specific benefits (60 seconds, 4-day results) | Soft CTAs | One clear message
❌ DON'T: Lead with brand name | Sound corporate | Make hook feel like an ad | Vague claims | Panic CTAs | Three competing messages

---

## TESTING FRAMEWORK

Run 3 variations per product: Emotional / Specific / Social proof
Test 7 days equal budget. Winner runs full budget. Analyze losing copy for next round.
