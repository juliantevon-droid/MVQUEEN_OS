# 🎁 MVQUEEN — Giveaway Systems

---

## Purpose

Run giveaways that build genuine community, grow a qualified audience, and reinforce brand positioning — not giveaways that attract freebie-seekers who disappear after winning.

**MVQUEEN giveaway rule:** Every giveaway should attract the exact customer we want — not just anyone who wants free stuff.

---

## Giveaway Types

| Type | Goal | Prize | Entry Method |
|------|------|-------|-------------|
| Community celebration | Reward existing followers | Hero product or bundle | Like + follow + tag |
| Launch amplification | Drive awareness for new product | New product featured | Follow + share to stories |
| UGC giveaway | Generate authentic content | Product bundle | Post with branded hashtag |
| Email list growth | Build owned audience | High-value bundle | Email signup |
| Milestone celebration | Community appreciation | Luxury bundle | Engagement-based |

## Giveaway Architecture

### Pre-Launch (5-7 days before)
```
□ Define goal — what does success look like beyond entries?
□ Select prize — must be desirable enough to share
□ Draft legal copy (no purchase necessary, open to residents of [X], void where prohibited)
□ Create announcement graphics
□ Plan entry mechanic — simple enough to complete, intentional enough to qualify
□ Set end date and winner selection method
□ Prepare winner notification DM template
```

### Launch Day
```
□ Post announcement with clear entry instructions
□ Stories series explaining the giveaway
□ Email segment notification (if applicable)
□ Pin announcement post to profile
```

### During Giveaway (Daily)
```
□ Engage with everyone who enters — like their tags, reply to comments
□ Post reminder stories at day 3 and day 6
□ Share UGC entries to stories (with permission)
□ Monitor for fake accounts or spam entries
```

### Close and Winner Selection
```
□ Use random selection tool (not manual — transparent)
□ Verify winner account is genuine
□ DM winner within 24 hours
□ Announce winner publicly (with their permission)
□ Fulfill prize within 5 business days
```

## Entry Mechanics by Goal

**Goal: Follower growth**
Entry: Follow + like post + tag 1 friend
Bonus entry: Share to stories

**Goal: Email list growth**
Entry: Sign up at link in bio
Bonus entry: Tag a friend in comments

**Goal: UGC generation**
Entry: Post a photo using [branded hashtag]
No follow required — lowers barrier, increases content volume

**Goal: Community reward**
Entry: Comment with answer to a question
Creates engagement signal to algorithm

## Prize Strategy

**What makes a good MVQUEEN giveaway prize:**
- High perceived value (not just high retail value)
- Reflects brand identity — not just "a bundle of stuff"
- Something she would have bought anyway
- Presented beautifully — even in a photo

**Prize tiers:**
| Tier | Value | Contents |
|------|-------|---------|
| Starter | $50-100 | 2-3 hero products |
| Ritual | $100-200 | Full ritual bundle + packaging |
| Luxury | $200-300 | Full collection + exclusive packaging + card |

---

## Giveaway Copy Template

**Caption formula:**
```
[Opening — atmosphere or brand truth]
[The gift — describe it like you love it]
[Why we're giving it — real reason]
[Entry instructions — numbered, clear, simple]
[End date]
[Legal line]
```

Example opening: *"For the woman who deserves a ritual that's entirely her own — we're giving one away."*

---

## Post-Giveaway Actions

```
□ Thank all participants in stories
□ Analyze follower retention rate (how many unfollow after giveaway?)
□ Segment new followers for nurture sequence
□ Collect UGC entries for repurposing (with permission)
□ Log performance in Campaign Log database
```

**Health check:** If more than 30% of followers gained unfollow within 7 days — the prize attracted the wrong audience. Adjust for next giveaway.
