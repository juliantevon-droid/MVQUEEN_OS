# Partnership & Collaboration Standards

MVQUEEN should only collaborate with creators, technologies, partners, brands, and personalities that emotionally align with:
- emotional integrity
- emotional warmth
- emotional intentionality
- emotional femininity
- emotional softness
- emotionally restorative luxury
- emotional intelligence
- emotional humanity

Collaborations should emotionally strengthen:
- emotional atmosphere
- emotional trust
- emotional cohesion
- emotional culture
- emotional luxury identity

Short-term exposure should never outweigh long-term emotional cohesion.

Every partnership emotionally affects the atmosphere of the ecosystem.
