# 🤝 MVQUEEN — Affiliate Program

---

## Purpose

Build a performance-based affiliate ecosystem that extends MVQUEEN's reach through aligned creators, customers, and communities — while maintaining brand integrity and generating measurable revenue.

---

## Affiliate Program Architecture

### Program Tiers

| Tier | Who | Commission | Perks |
|------|-----|-----------|-------|
| **Brand Ambassador** | Loyal customers, micro-creators (1K-10K) | 15% | Early access, gifting, personal code |
| **Creator Partner** | Content creators (10K-100K) | 20% | Monthly gifting, dedicated support, performance bonuses |
| **Elite Partner** | Established influencers (100K+) | Custom | Full partnership, co-creation opportunities |

---

## How the Program Works

**Tracking:** Unique affiliate link + discount code per affiliate
**Cookie window:** 30 days (customer has 30 days after clicking to purchase)
**Payout:** Monthly, minimum $50 threshold, via PayPal or bank transfer
**Dashboard:** Affiliates access their own dashboard showing clicks, conversions, earnings

---

## Affiliate Application Process

```
1. Applicant submits application (form on website or via DM)
2. Review against criteria (below) — 48 hour response
3. Approved: Onboarding email with welcome kit, code, link, and brand guidelines
4. Declined: Warm response — invite to reapply after 3 months
```

**Application criteria:**
| Criteria | Minimum Standard |
|----------|----------------|
| Aesthetic alignment | Feminine, soft, elevated — not jarring contrast |
| Audience fit | Primarily women 20-45 |
| Engagement quality | Real engagement, not inflated |
| Content quality | Consistent, intentional, brand-adjacent |
| Values alignment | No content that contradicts MVQUEEN values |

---

## Affiliate Onboarding Kit

Every approved affiliate receives:
```
□ Welcome message — personal, warm
□ Unique discount code (10% off for their audience)
□ Unique tracking link
□ Brand guidelines PDF (condensed — voice, visuals, dos/don'ts)
□ Product knowledge guide
□ Content ideas (suggestions, never scripts)
□ First product shipment — for genuine use and content
□ Dashboard access link
```

---

## Affiliate Content Guidelines

**They should:**
- Share honest experiences with products
- Use their natural voice — not MVQUEEN's voice
- Disclose the partnership (#ad or #affiliate per FTC rules)
- Share their real ritual and how MVQUEEN fits it

**They should not:**
- Make medical or therapeutic claims
- Compare MVQUEEN negatively to competitors
- Use aggressive discount or urgency language
- Post content that contradicts brand values
- Share internal pricing or supplier information

---

## Affiliate Performance Management

| Metric | Review | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Clicks | Monthly | Low clicks = content support needed |
| Conversion rate | Monthly | Low CVR = audience mismatch or code issue |
| Revenue generated | Monthly | Top performers → upgrade tier |
| Content quality | Quarterly | Off-brand content → coaching or exit |
| Activity level | Quarterly | Inactive 90 days → check in or offboard |

**Top affiliate rewards:**
- Bonus commission on milestone months
- Exclusive product access before launch
- Feature on MVQUEEN's main channels
- Co-creation invitation for high performers
