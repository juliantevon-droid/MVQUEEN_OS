# 🗓️ MVQUEEN — Seasonal Campaigns

---

## Purpose

Plan and execute seasonal campaigns that feel culturally relevant and emotionally resonant — without abandoning brand identity to chase every trend or holiday.

**MVQUEEN seasonal rule:** Every seasonal campaign must connect to a genuine brand truth. Not just "it's Valentine's Day, here's a sale."

## Tier 1 Campaigns (Full Production)

These campaigns receive full creative treatment: email sequence, social series, paid ads, product features, possible limited edition.

**Valentine's Day — "Love Yourself First"**
Theme: Feminine self-love — not performative, not dependent on a partner
Products: Skincare ritual sets, fragrances, anything she'd buy for herself
Copy angle: *"She doesn't wait to be given something luxurious. She chooses it herself."*

**Mother's Day — "The Women Who Taught Us Softness"**
Theme: Tribute to feminine legacy — mothers, mentors, the women who shaped us
Products: Gift sets, luxury bundles, anything giftable with beautiful packaging
Copy angle: *"For the woman who showed you what strength looked like when it was soft."*

**Holiday / December — "Give Her Something That Means Something"**
Theme: Intentional gifting — against the noise of mass consumerism
Products: Full gift sets, luxury bundles, wrapping and presentation elevated
Copy angle: *"Not just a gift. A ritual she'll carry into the new year."*

**New Year — "The Ritual of Becoming"**
Theme: Intentional new beginning — not resolution culture, but ritual-setting
Products: Full ritual kits, skincare systems, anything that signals a fresh start
Copy angle: *"Not new year, new you. New year, more you."*

---

## Tier 2 Campaigns (Lighter Touch)

These campaigns receive: 1-2 emails, social content series, no paid ads unless performing.

- Spring launch: New season, new ritual, fresh arrivals
- Summer: Slow down, simplify, summer skin/hair focus
- Fall: Deeper rituals, richer textures, cozy femininity
- Back to ritual (September): End of summer, rebuilding routines
