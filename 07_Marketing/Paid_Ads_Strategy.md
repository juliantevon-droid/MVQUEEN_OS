# Paid Ads Strategy: The Emotional Acquisition Loop
## MVQUEEN_OS / 07_Marketing

---

## 1. Philosophy
We do not "sell" products through ads. We invite women into an emotional world. Our ads should feel like a restorative pause in a busy feed.

---

## 2. Platform Strategy

### Meta (Instagram/Facebook)
*   **Objective:** Top-of-funnel (TOF) brand awareness and conversion.
*   **Creative:** High-aesthetic lifestyle imagery and cinematic video.
*   **Copy:** Focus on the "Softness" and "Luxury" pillars.

### TikTok
*   **Objective:** Virality and rapid acquisition.
*   **Creative:** Lo-fi, authentic, "Get Ready With Me" (GRWM) style content featuring MVQueen pieces.
*   **Copy:** Relatable, identity-driven hooks.

---

## 3. Funnel Structure
1.  **TOF (Cold):** Broad targeting focusing on "Luxury Lifestyle" and "Self-Care" interests.
2.  **MOF (Warm):** Retargeting video viewers and social engagers with social proof and testimonials.
3.  **BOF (Hot):** Retargeting website visitors and cart abandoners with a soft incentive (e.g., "Complete your sanctuary").

---

## 4. Creative Testing Protocol
*   Test 3 new hooks every week.
*   Test 2 new visual styles (Cinematic vs. Authentic) every month.
*   Kill any ad with a ROAS below 2.0x after $100 spend.
