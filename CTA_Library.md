# 👑 MVQUEEN CTA LIBRARY
*The complete call-to-action system for every MVQUEEN touchpoint*

---

## PURPOSE

A CTA is not just a button. For MVQUEEN, every call to action is an invitation — a moment where the brand reaches out its hand and says: *come with me.*

MVQUEEN CTAs must feel like an extension of the brand voice: warm, confident, feminine, and slightly aspirational. They move her forward without pressure. They invite without desperation. They make clicking feel like a choice she's proud of.

---

## CTA PRINCIPLES

1. Invitation over command — ❌ "BUY NOW" → ✅ "Shop the Collection"
2. Identity-forward language — ❌ "Add to Cart" → ✅ "Claim Yours"
3. Specificity over generic — ❌ "Learn More" → ✅ "Read Her Story"
4. Softness and confidence in balance

## CTA TONE REFERENCE

| Situation | Tone | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Hero banner | Confident, aspirational | "Enter the World of MVQUEEN" |
| Product page | Warm, direct | "Make It Mine" |
| Email signup | Inviting, exclusive | "Join the Reign" |
| Social caption | Conversational, feminine | "Link in bio, queen." |
| Abandoned cart | Warm, no pressure | "Your Bag Is Still Waiting" |
| Sale/promo | Elevated, never desperate | "Yours — While She Lasts" |
| VIP/loyalty | Exclusive, celebratory | "You've Earned This" |
| Re-engagement | Warm, personal | "We Saved Something for You" |

---

## WORDS NEVER USED IN CTAs
❌ "Click here" | ❌ "Hurry!" | ❌ "Act fast!" | ❌ "Cheap" | ❌ "Affordable"
❌ "You won't regret it" | ❌ "What are you waiting for?"
