# Content Pipeline Architecture
## MVQUEEN_OS / 33_Content_Pipelines

---

## 1. Purpose
This document defines the automated and manual workflows that move content from "Idea" to "Published Asset" while maintaining 100% doctrine alignment.

---

## 2. The Pipeline Stages

### Stage 1: Ideation (AI-Assisted)
*   **Input:** Current brand themes and seasonal trends.
*   **Process:** `Content_Agent` generates 20 content ideas per week.
*   **Output:** Approved ideas in `99_Command_Center/Content_Calendar.md`.

### Stage 2: Production (Human-Led)
*   **Process:** Weekly batch filming session (see `Weekly_Content_Batch_SOP`).
*   **Output:** Raw video and photo assets.

### Stage 3: Post-Production (AI-Assisted)
*   **Process:** `Content_Agent` generates captions, hooks, and alt-text.
*   **Output:** Final assets ready for scheduling.

### Stage 4: Distribution (Automated)
*   **Process:** Assets uploaded to scheduling tools (Later, Planoly, or Shopify Flow).
*   **Output:** Live posts on IG, TikTok, and Pinterest.

---

## 3. Quality Gates
1.  **Visual Gate:** Does the asset match the `02_Brand_Identity` color system?
2.  **Voice Gate:** Does the caption pass the `Forbidden_Words.md` check?
3.  **Strategic Gate:** Does this post support one of the 4 Emotional Pillars?
