# 👥 MVQUEEN — Team Structure

---

## Purpose

Define the organizational structure of MVQUEEN at every growth stage — who does what, how decisions are made, and how the team scales without losing brand integrity.

---

## Current Structure (Solo / Early Stage)

```
FOUNDER
├── Brand Strategy & Creative Direction
├── Product Sourcing & Management
├── Content Creation
├── Marketing & Campaigns
├── Customer Service
├── Operations & Fulfillment
└── Finance & Analytics
```

**AI assistance handles:**
- Content drafts (reviewed by founder)
- Product copy generation (reviewed by founder)
- SEO metadata (reviewed by founder)
- Email drafts (reviewed by founder)
- Analytics summaries (reviewed by founder)

---

## Growth Stage Structure ($5k-$20k/month)

**First hire priority: Virtual Assistant**

```
FOUNDER (Strategy + Creative + Product)
├── VA — Operations, customer service, scheduling, admin
└── Freelance Creator — Content production (as needed)
```

**VA responsibilities:**
- Order management and customer service (with scripts)
- Social media scheduling (not creation)
- Supplier follow-ups
- Data entry and reporting
- Email list management

---

## Scaling Stage Structure ($20k-$50k/month)

```
FOUNDER (Vision + Brand Direction + Product)
├── Operations Manager
│   ├── Fulfillment coordination
│   └── Customer service team (1-2 reps)
├── Marketing Lead
│   ├── Email + SMS campaigns
│   └── Paid ads management
└── Content Creator (Full or part-time)
    ├── Social content
    └── Photography/video
```

---

## Expansion Stage Structure ($50k+/month)

```
FOUNDER / CEO
├── Creative Director
│   ├── Photographer/Videographer
│   └── Graphic Designer
├── Marketing Director
│   ├── Paid Ads Specialist
│   ├── Social Media Manager
│   └── Email Marketing Manager
├── Operations Director
│   ├── Inventory Manager
│   └── Customer Experience Lead
└── AI Systems Manager
    ├── Prompt Library Management
    └── Agent Oversight
```

---

## Role Hiring Criteria

Every MVQUEEN hire — regardless of role — must:

| Criteria | Standard |
|----------|----------|
| Brand alignment | Understands and respects the brand philosophy |
| Communication | Clear, professional, responsive |
| Reliability | Delivers what they say, when they say |
| Quality standard | Takes pride in work — notices details |
| NDA compliance | Signs and respects confidentiality |

**Hiring order of priority:**
1. VA (operations relief)
2. Customer service rep (relationship protection)
3. Content creator (growth fuel)
4. Marketing manager (revenue acceleration)
5. Operations manager (scaling infrastructure)

---

## Decision Authority Matrix

| Decision Type | Solo Stage | Growth Stage | Scaling Stage |
|--------------|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| Brand direction | Founder | Founder only | Founder only |
| Product decisions | Founder | Founder only | Founder + Product Lead |
| Campaign strategy | Founder | Founder + Marketing | Marketing Director |
| Customer resolution | Founder | VA (scripts) + escalate | CS Lead + escalate |
| Content approval | Founder | Founder reviews | Creative Director |
| Financial decisions | Founder | Founder only | Founder + Ops Director |

**Rule:** Brand and product decisions never delegate below Creative Director level.
