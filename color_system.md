# 👑 MVQUEEN COLOR SYSTEM
*The complete color language of the MVQUEEN brand ecosystem*

---

## PURPOSE

Color is not decoration. For MVQUEEN, color is emotional communication.

Every shade in this system was chosen to activate a specific feeling — softness, warmth, confidence, elegance, radiance. Color is how the brand speaks before a single word is read. It is the first emotional signal a woman receives, and it must feel instantly luxurious, feminine, and safe.

This system governs every visual touchpoint: website, packaging, social media, email, campaigns, and product presentation.

---

## THE MVQUEEN COLOR PHILOSOPHY

MVQUEEN color exists on a spectrum of **warm feminine luxury**.

The palette is never cold. Never harsh. Never clinical.

It lives in the space between:
- champagne and cream
- blush and rose
- warm nude and soft brown
- deep plum and rich berry
- black and charcoal (used sparingly, always warmed)
- gold and bronze (never silver as primary)

The emotional register of every color choice must answer:
**Does this make her feel beautiful, soft, and elevated?**

If yes — it belongs.
If no — it does not.

## COLOR PAIRING RULES

### Primary Pairings (Always Safe)
- CHAMPAGNE IVORY + QUEEN NOIR + CHAMPAGNE GOLD
- CHAMPAGNE IVORY + BLUSH REIGN + CHAMPAGNE GOLD
- QUEEN NOIR + CHAMPAGNE GOLD + ROSE SMOKE

### Never Pair
- BLUSH REIGN + bright white (too stark, loses warmth)
- CHAMPAGNE GOLD + silver (mixed metal energy)
- DEEP PLUM + PRINCESS PINK (too much contrast, brand confusion)
- Any neon or cool-toned color with the MVQUEEN palette
