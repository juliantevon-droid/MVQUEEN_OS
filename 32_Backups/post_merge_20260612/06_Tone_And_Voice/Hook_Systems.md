# 🪝 MVQUEEN — Hook Systems

---

## What Makes a Hook Work

A hook has one job: stop her mid-scroll and make her feel something in under 3 seconds.

Not inform. Not sell. **Feel.**

The best MVQUEEN hooks create a micro-moment of recognition —
she sees herself, her desire, or her problem reflected back at her
before she even realizes she stopped scrolling.

---

## The 12 MVQUEEN Hook Formulas

---

### Formula 01 — The Identity Mirror
Reflect who she already is or who she is becoming.
She stops because she sees herself.

**Structure:** `For the woman who ___`

**Examples:**
- *For the woman who treats softness like a discipline.*
- *For the woman who is done performing and ready to just live.*
- *For the woman building a life that feels as good as it looks.*
- *For the woman who romanticizes everything — and isn't sorry.*

---

### Formula 02 — The Quiet Disruption
Challenge a common belief without being aggressive.
She stops because something feels true that she never heard said out loud.

**Structure:** `[Common assumption] is a lie. / Nobody talks about ___`

**Examples:**
- *Luxury isn't supposed to feel intimidating.*
- *Softness is not something you grow out of.*
- *Nobody talks about how beauty should restore you — not exhaust you.*
- *Self-care wasn't supposed to become another thing to optimize.*

---

### Formula 03 — The Sensory Open
Drop her into a feeling before she knows what you're selling.
She stops because she wants to stay in the feeling.

**Structure:** `[Sensory moment] — [what it means]`

**Examples:**
- *The kind of morning where everything feels intentional.*
- *Skin so soft you forget to overthink.*
- *That feeling when your space finally matches who you're becoming.*
- *Slow mornings. Good products. Nothing to prove.*

---

### Formula 04 — The Honest Admission
Be real in a way other brands aren't.
She stops because it sounds human — not marketing.

**Structure:** `We made this because ___` / `Honestly, ___`

**Examples:**
- *We made this for the days when you need beauty to do the emotional heavy lifting.*
- *Honestly? Most luxury brands weren't built for women like you.*
- *We didn't create another skincare routine. We created a ritual.*

---

### Formula 05 — The Before State
Name exactly where she is right now.
She stops because she feels understood.

**Structure:** `If you've ever felt ___ / been tired of ___`

**Examples:**
- *If you've ever bought something beautiful and felt nothing.*
- *If you're tired of beauty routines that feel like chores.*
- *If you've been waiting for a brand that actually gets it.*
- *If "good enough" stopped being enough.*

---

### Formula 06 — The Transformation Tease
Show the after without giving away the how.
She stops because she wants to know more.

**Structure:** `What if ___ felt different?`

**Examples:**
- *What if your morning routine felt like the best part of your day?*
- *What if luxury actually felt like it was made for you?*
- *What if softness was something you could wear?*

---

### Formula 07 — The Specificity Hook
Be so specific she thinks you know her personally.
She stops because it's too accurate to ignore.

**Structure:** `[Very specific scenario she recognizes]`

**Examples:**
- *The candle. The silk pillowcase. The skincare you actually use.*
- *Not the "treat yourself" spiral. The intentional kind of self-care.*
- *The version of you that takes her time. She deserves better products.*

---

### Formula 08 — The Contrast Hook
Put two opposites next to each other.
She stops because the tension is interesting.

**Structure:** `[Contrast A] not [Contrast B]`

**Examples:**
- *Soft, not weak. Elevated, not unreachable.*
- *Made for real life — not a photoshoot.*
- *Luxury that feels lived-in, not staged.*
- *Feminine without the performance.*

---

### Formula 09 — The Permission Hook
Give her permission she didn't know she needed.
She stops because it feels like relief.

**Structure:** `You're allowed to ___`

**Examples:**
- *You're allowed to want things that feel beautiful.*
- *You're allowed to take up space softly.*
- *You're allowed to choose luxury without justifying it.*
- *You're allowed to romanticize your everyday life.*

---

### Formula 10 — The Number Hook
Use a specific number to signal value.
She stops because specificity implies expertise.

**Structure:** `[Number] things / ways / reasons`

**Examples:**
- *3 things in your routine that should feel like rituals, not tasks.*
- *The one product my shelf hasn't been without in 6 months.*
- *5 signs your self-care has become another performance.*

---

### Formula 11 — The Question Hook
Ask the question she's already asking herself.
She stops because she wants the answer.

**Structure:** `[Question she already has]`

**Examples:**
- *Why does skincare feel like a chore when it should feel like a ritual?*
- *What does luxury actually feel like when it's made for you?*
- *Is it possible to feel elevated without spending thousands?*

---

### Formula 12 — The Statement Hook
A single declarative sentence with quiet power.
She stops because it sounds like a truth she hasn't heard yet.

**Structure:** `[One powerful declarative sentence]`

**Examples:**
- *Softness is not something you outgrow.*
- *The right product doesn't complete you. It honors you.*
- *Beauty is not a performance. It's a private ritual.*
- *Luxury should feel like breathing, not achieving.*

## Hook Length by Platform

| Platform | Hook Length | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| TikTok | 1-2 sentences max | First 2 seconds are everything |
| Instagram Reel | 1 sentence on screen | Text overlay must be instant |
| Instagram Caption | First line before "more" | Under 125 characters |
| Email Subject | 6-9 words | Curiosity or identity-driven |
| Ad Primary Text | First sentence | Under 20 words |
| Pinterest | First line of description | Keyword-aware |
| SMS | Entire message IS the hook | Under 160 chars |

## Hook Testing Protocol

Test every hook before scaling:

1. Read it out loud — does it sound human?
2. Does it make you feel something in under 5 seconds?
3. Could any other brand have written this? (If yes — rewrite)
4. Is it leading with feeling or information?
5. Would she stop scrolling for this?

Run 2-3 hook variations per piece of content.
Track which hooks drive the most saves, shares, and link clicks.
Build a swipe file of top performers in `13_Research_And_Inspiration/Swipe_Files.md`
