# 🎙️ MVQUEEN — Platform Voice Guides

---

## How to Use This Document

Each platform has its own native language — its own pace, intimacy level, and format.
MVQUEEN's voice stays consistent across all platforms,
but the *expression* of that voice adapts to where it lives.

Same soul. Different register.

---

## Product Description Voice

### The Standard
Product descriptions are where brand philosophy meets commerce.
They must feel beautiful and convert — simultaneously.

**Voice register:** Sensory, aspirational, intimate
**Sentence length:** Mix of short punches and flowing atmosphere
**Perspective:** Second person (you/your) or third person (she/her)
**Never:** Feature lists without emotional translation

### The Formula
Lead with feeling → Deliver sensory detail → Name the transformation → Close with invitation

### What It Sounds Like

**❌ Wrong:**
*Hydrating face cream with hyaluronic acid and vitamin E. Suitable for all skin types. Apply morning and night.*

**✅ Right:**
*She sinks in on contact — no waiting, no heaviness, just skin that finally feels like skin.*
*The kind of softness that stays past noon, past the meeting, past the moment you stopped thinking about it.*
*Morning or night. However she fits into your ritual.*

### Product Description Structure
```
Line 1-2: Sensory opening — what it feels/smells/looks like
Lines 3-4: The transformation — what changes after consistent use
Lines 5-6: Who it's for — identity framing
Line 7: Usage — practical but written with warmth
```

## Email Voice

### The Standard
Email is the most intimate channel MVQUEEN has.
She gave us her inbox — that's not nothing.
Write every email like a personal letter, not a broadcast.

**Voice register:** Personal, warm, conversational luxury
**Length:** Long enough to feel complete, short enough to finish
**Perspective:** Always second person (you)
**Never:** Generic openers, robotic urgency, mass-broadcast tone

### What It Sounds Like

**❌ Wrong:**
*Hi [First Name], Don't miss our HUGE sale! Up to 40% off everything. Shop now before it ends!*

**✅ Right:**
*Some things are worth the pause.*
*We've been working on [Product Name] for longer than we planned — because we kept going until it felt right.*
*It's here now. And we wanted you to have it first.*

### Email Structure Principles
- Open with atmosphere or a real human moment — never a sale announcement
- Get to the point by paragraph 2 — she has a life
- One primary CTA per email — never three competing asks
- Close warmly — not clinically
- Subject lines: curiosity or identity-driven, never all-caps urgency

### Subject Line Voice
| Tone | Example |
|------|---------|
| Curiosity | *Something we've been keeping.*|
| Identity | *For the version of you who's ready.* |
| Intimacy | *This one's personal.* |
| Announcement | *She's finally here.* |
| Gratitude | *Because you deserve to know first.* |

## SMS Voice

### The Standard
SMS is the most direct channel. She sees it on her lock screen.
It must feel like a message from someone who respects her attention.

**Voice register:** Brief, warm, human — never robotic
**Length:** Under 160 characters ideally, 320 max
**Perspective:** Second person
**Never:** Aggressive urgency, emojis that cheapen the brand, generic copy

### What It Sounds Like

**❌ Wrong:**
*SALE ENDS TONIGHT! 40% OFF! SHOP NOW: [link]*

**✅ Right:**
*Your ritual is one tap away. New arrivals just dropped — made for you. [link]*

### SMS Templates by Type

**Order confirmation:**
*Your MVQUEEN order is confirmed. We packed it with intention — she's on her way. 🤍*

**Shipping notification:**
*She's been shipped. Your [Product Name] is moving toward you. Track her here: [link]*

**Abandoned cart:**
*You left something behind. She's still here if you want her. [link]*

**New arrival:**
*Something new just arrived at MVQUEEN — and it has your name on it. [link]*

**Flash moment (not sale, but launch):**
*[Product Name] is live. First access, just for you. [link]*

## Social Voice

### The Standard
Social media is where MVQUEEN builds its world publicly.
The voice must feel human enough to stop a scroll
and elevated enough to belong to a luxury brand.

**Voice register:** Intimate, warm, visually aware — like a woman speaking to one friend
**Platform variations:** (see below)
**Never:** Corporate speak, aggressive selling in captions, overused trend language

### Platform Breakdown

**Instagram:**
- Aspirational but personal
- Caption opens strong — first line must work standalone
- Mix of short punches and atmospheric longer copy
- Community-forward — ask questions, invite responses
- Hashtags: 8-12, mix of branded and discovery

**TikTok:**
- More raw and real — less polished
- Hook in first 2 seconds — on screen text matters
- Educational, relatable, or transformation-driven
- Comments are content — engage back
- Trends are filtered through brand identity before adoption

**Pinterest:**
- SEO-forward — keywords woven naturally into descriptions
- Descriptive and atmospheric
- Longer descriptions perform better here
- No urgency language — Pinterest is for saving, not impulse

**Facebook:**
- Slightly longer form than Instagram
- Community-building and story-driven
- Good for longer brand narratives and customer stories

## Ad Copy Voice

### The Standard
Ad copy is MVQUEEN's first impression for thousands of women
who have never heard of the brand.
It must be sharp, true, and impossible to forget.

**Voice register:** Precise, identity-driven, emotionally intelligent
**Length:** Short — every word must earn its place
**Never:** Feature lists, discount-first language, aggressive urgency

### Ad Copy by Format

**Primary Text (Meta Feed):**
2-3 sentences max. Lead with identity or transformation. End with brand.

**Headline (Meta):**
4-8 words. Should work as a standalone declaration.

**Story/Reel Overlay:**
One line. Maximum impact. Reads in under 2 seconds.

### Ad Copy Examples

**Awareness ad:**
*Primary:* For the woman who chooses softness on purpose. MVQUEEN — luxury feminine living.
*Headline:* Softness is a standard.

**Conversion ad:**
*Primary:* She goes on clean, feels weightless, stays all day. [Product Name] — now in the link.
*Headline:* Made for your ritual.

**Retargeting ad:**
*Primary:* You already know you want it. [Product Name] is still here.
*Headline:* She's waiting for you.

## Blog Voice

### The Standard
Blog content is where MVQUEEN demonstrates expertise and builds SEO authority.
It must rank AND read beautifully — never choose one over the other.

**Voice register:** Thoughtful, editorial, warm expert
**Length:** 1,200-2,500 words depending on topic
**Structure:** Woman-first opening → Value delivery → Brand connection → Invitation close
**Never:** Robotic keyword stuffing, clinical how-to language without warmth

### Blog Voice Characteristics
- Speaks to her as a woman — not a search query
- Gives real, specific advice — not vague generalities
- Weaves brand philosophy in naturally — not as a sales pitch at the end
- Uses headers that create curiosity — not just categories
- Short paragraphs — this is read on mobile

### Blog Opener Formula
*Don't open with a definition or a statistic.*
Open with a moment she recognizes.

**❌ Wrong:** *Skincare has been an important part of beauty routines for centuries...*
**✅ Right:** *There's a version of your morning that doesn't feel like a checklist. This is how to find it.*

## SEO Voice

### The Standard
SEO content at MVQUEEN is brand-first, search-aware.
Keywords are woven — never stuffed.
Every SEO piece must pass the brand voice test AND the search intent test.

**Voice register:** Educational, authoritative, warm — same as blog voice
**Keyword approach:** Primary keyword in title, first 100 words, and 2-3 headers naturally
**Never:** Keyword repetition that sounds robotic, thin content that ranks but doesn't deliver value

### SEO Metadata Voice

**Meta title formula:**
`[Primary keyword] — [Brand value or promise] | MVQUEEN`
Example: *Luxury Skincare for Women — Rituals That Restore | MVQUEEN*

**Meta description formula:**
`[Keyword-aware opening] + [Emotional promise] + [Soft CTA]`
Example: *Discover luxury skincare built for real women. Rituals that restore, products that feel intentional. Shop MVQUEEN.*

### SEO Content Quality Check
```
□ Primary keyword in H1?
□ Keyword appears naturally in first 100 words?
□ Secondary keywords in H2s where relevant?
□ Does it read like MVQUEEN — not a generic SEO article?
□ Does it deliver real value to a real reader?
□ Meta title under 60 characters?
□ Meta description under 160 characters?
□ Internal links to relevant MVQUEEN pages?
```
