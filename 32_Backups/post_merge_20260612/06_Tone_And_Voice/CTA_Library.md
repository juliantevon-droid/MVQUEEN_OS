# 👑 MVQUEEN — CTA Library

---

## CTA Philosophy

MVQUEEN CTAs feel like invitations — not commands.

The difference between a CTA that converts and one that repels
is the difference between *"Buy Now"* and *"She deserves this."*

Every CTA should make her feel like she's choosing something for herself —
not being pushed toward a transaction.

**The rule:** Every CTA ends a feeling — it does not start a sale.

## CTA Formulas

### Formula 01 — The Identity CTA
Connect the action to who she is.
`[Action] + [identity statement]`

*Shop the ritual. You've earned this version of yourself.*
*Claim yours. Soft women deserve soft things.*

### Formula 02 — The Permission CTA
Give her permission to act.
`You're allowed to [desire]. [Action].`

*You're allowed to want this. Shop now.*
*You deserve the ritual. Start here.*

### Formula 03 — The Urgency-Without-Pressure CTA
Create desire without manufactured panic.
`[Soft scarcity] + [invitation]`

*She won't be here forever. Make her yours.*
*Limited first batch — yours while she lasts.*

### Formula 04 — The Curiosity CTA
Make her want to know more.
`[Question or tease] → [Action]`

*Wondering what the ritual feels like? Tap to find out.*
*She's been our best-kept secret. Until now. Shop.*

## CTAs We Never Use

| Avoid | Why |
|-------|-----|
| SHOP NOW (all caps) | Aggressive, demand-based |
| BUY NOW | Transactional, no feeling |
| LIMITED TIME OFFER!!! | Manufactured panic |
| Don't miss out! | Pressure-based, not invitation |
| Act fast! | Desperate energy |
| Click here | Generic, no brand voice |
| Add to cart | Functional only, no emotional context |
