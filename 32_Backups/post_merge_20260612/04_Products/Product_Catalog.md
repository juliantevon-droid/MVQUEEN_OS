# 19. Product Ecosystem

## Product Ecosystem Philosophy

MVQUEEN products are not designed to exist as isolated items.

They are designed to function as an emotionally connected feminine luxury ecosystem that supports beauty, confidence, emotional atmosphere, intentional living, softness, self-expression, sensory immersion, and emotionally elevated experiences.

The purpose of the MVQUEEN product ecosystem is not simply to sell products.

It is to build an emotionally immersive world women emotionally attach themselves to.

Every category should feel emotionally interconnected through:
- femininity
- emotional softness
- sensory luxury
- emotional warmth
- immersive beauty
- confidence
- intentional presentation
- emotional atmosphere
- cinematic storytelling
- emotionally intelligent luxury

The ecosystem should feel emotionally cohesive rather than commercially assembled.

Women should feel like every product belongs to the same emotional universe.

## Catalog Maintenance Protocol

- New product added to catalog BEFORE Shopify listing is created
- SKU assigned from `SKU_Systems.md` on catalog entry
- Status updated within 24 hours of any change
- Discontinued products moved to archive section — SKU never reassigned
- Full database entry created in `37_Databases/DB-01` on catalog addition
