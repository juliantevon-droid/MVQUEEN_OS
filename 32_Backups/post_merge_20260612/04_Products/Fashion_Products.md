# 👗 MVQUEEN — Fashion Products

---

## Purpose

Define the fashion product strategy, aesthetic standards, sourcing philosophy, and presentation framework for MVQUEEN's clothing and fashion category.

---

## Fashion Category Philosophy

MVQUEEN fashion is not trend-driven.
It is identity-driven.

The woman who shops MVQUEEN fashion is not asking *"What's in season?"*
She is asking *"What feels like me — the version of me I'm becoming?"*

MVQUEEN fashion serves:
- The intentional wardrobe builder
- The woman who buys fewer, better things
- The woman who wants to feel elevated in her everyday life — not just for occasions
- The woman who values how clothing feels as much as how it looks

---

## Fashion Aesthetic Standards

### Silhouettes
- Feminine without being restrictive
- Flattering across body types — never designed for one silhouette only
- Classic over trend — pieces that work for 3+ years
- Movement-friendly — she has a life to live in these clothes

### Fabrics
- Prioritize feel over appearance
- Natural and semi-natural fabrics preferred: cotton, linen, silk, viscose, modal
- Softness is a selection criterion — does it feel good against skin?
- Durability matters — luxury means it lasts

### Colors
- Aligned with brand color system: neutrals, soft tones, feminine accents
- Versatile — pieces that work with what she already owns
- Seasonally appropriate but not trend-dependent

### Details
- Thoughtful, not excessive
- Details that serve function and beauty simultaneously
- Nothing that screams "trend" — subtlety signals quality

## Fashion Sourcing Standards

Every fashion piece sourced for MVQUEEN must meet:

```
□ Fabric quality test — feel it, wear it, wash it before ordering
□ Sizing accuracy — compare to standard measurements
□ Construction quality — seams, finishing, hardware
□ Photography sample ordered before full inventory commitment
□ MOQ manageable for test run
□ Lead time confirmed and acceptable
□ Returns process with supplier agreed
```

**Rule:** Order 10-20 units as a test run before committing to full inventory. Prove the product before scaling the investment.

---

## Fashion Photography Standards

Fashion requires lifestyle photography — not just product shots.

**Styling direction:**
- Styled with complementary MVQUEEN accessories where possible
- Natural, atmospheric settings — not white-wall studio only
- Model styling: natural hair and makeup, relaxed expression
- Movement shots alongside static — show how it moves

**Shot list per fashion item:**
1. Full-length front — styled
2. Full-length back
3. Detail shot (fabric texture, closure, special detail)
4. Lifestyle shot — movement or atmospheric
5. Flat lay — brand aesthetic

## Fashion Copy Standards

- The occasions and moments it belongs in
- The identity it reflects
- The quality and durability signals
