# 5. The Trend Lifecycle (How Trends Move)

### **1. Emergence**
A small group of aesthetic leaders adopt it.

### **2. Resonance**
It emotionally aligns with a wider audience.

### **3. Acceleration**
It spreads through UGC, influencers, and aesthetic communities.

### **4. Saturation**
It becomes mainstream.

### **5. Evolution**
It transforms into a more refined version.

MVQUEEN focuses on **resonance → acceleration → evolution**.
