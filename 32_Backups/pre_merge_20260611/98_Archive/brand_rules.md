# 👑 MVQUEEN BRAND RULES
*The non-negotiable laws that govern every MVQUEEN expression*

---

## PURPOSE

Brand rules exist to protect the integrity of MVQUEEN across every touchpoint, every piece of content, every AI output, every campaign, and every team member's work.

These are not guidelines. They are laws.

---

## SECTION 1 — BRAND IDENTITY RULES

### R-01: The Name Is MVQUEEN — Always
✅ MVQUEEN | ❌ MV Queen | ❌ mvqueen | ❌ MV QUEEN (with space)
Possessive: MVQUEEN's

### R-02: The Sister Brand Is Miss.Princess — Always
✅ Miss.Princess | ❌ Miss Princess | ❌ MISS PRINCESS | ❌ Miss.princess

### R-03: The Full Meaning Is "Most Valuable Queen"
Never abbreviate as "MVQ"

### R-04: The Crown Symbol Is The Only Brand Icon
👑 — one per communication piece maximum in external content

### R-05: MVQUEEN and Miss.Princess Are Always Distinct
- MVQUEEN = mature, confident, elegant, elevated femininity
- Miss.Princess = youthful, playful, dreamy, soft glam

---

## SECTION 2 — VISUAL IDENTITY RULES

### R-06: Always Use The Approved Color Palette
Primary: QUEEN NOIR · CHAMPAGNE IVORY · BLUSH REIGN · CHAMPAGNE GOLD · VELVET ROSE
Secondary: WARM NUDE · SOFT MAUVE · DEEP PLUM · ROSE SMOKE · BRONZE LIGHT

❌ No bright whites | ❌ No cool tones | ❌ No neons | ❌ No off-palette choices

### R-07: Always Use The Approved Font System
Display: Cormorant Garamond | Body: Jost | Fallback: Georgia / Arial
❌ No Roboto | ❌ No Open Sans | ❌ No more than 3 fonts per layout

### R-08: Photography Must Always Feel Warm, Soft, and Elevated
Warm lighting, approved background colors, aspirational but accessible

### R-09: Packaging Is A Brand Experience, Not Just A Container
Base: CHAMPAGNE IVORY or QUEEN NOIR | Foil: CHAMPAGNE GOLD
Tissue: ROSE SMOKE | Ribbon: BLUSH REIGN or CHAMPAGNE GOLD
Include: Personal brand-voice note inside

---

## SECTION 3 — VOICE AND LANGUAGE RULES

### R-10: Always Write In The MVQUEEN Voice
Warm · elegant · confident · emotionally intelligent · feminine

### R-11: She Is Always "She" — Never Generic
Never: "the customer" / "users" / "buyers" / "people" / "consumers"

### R-12: The Brand Speaks To Her, Not About Her
✅ "You deserve this." | ❌ "Women will love this product."

### R-13: Forbidden Words Are Absolute
See Forbidden_Words.md — no exceptions.

### R-14: "Emotional" Is A Precise Tool, Not A Filler Word
Used deliberately and sparingly. Choose specific language instead:
- "emotional elevation" → "she feels more herself"
- "emotional connection" → "she feels seen"
- "emotionally empowered" → "she walks differently"

### R-15: The Brand Never Apologizes For Its Prices
✅ "Luxury that doesn't ask you to choose."
❌ "Affordable!" | ❌ "Low price!" | ❌ "Budget luxury"

---

## SECTION 4 — CONTENT RULES

### R-16: Every Piece Of Content Must Serve One Of The Three Purposes
1. Inspire — make her feel something
2. Educate — teach her something
3. Convert — move her toward action

### R-17: Content Must Always Reflect The Brand's Core Pillars
Confidence · Femininity · Elegance · Softness · Luxury · Self-worth · Transformation

### R-18: No Trend-Chasing That Dilutes Brand Identity
Participate only when the trend can be executed in MVQUEEN's tonal and visual language.

### R-19: AI-Generated Content Must Pass The Brand Filter
AI is a tool. The brand is the filter. Review before publishing.

---

## SECTION 5 — POSITIONING RULES

### R-20: MVQUEEN Is Accessible Luxury — Not Fast Fashion
Everything must signal premium quality and intentional design at every price point.

### R-21: The Brand Does Not Compete On Price — It Competes On Feeling
She chooses MVQUEEN for how it makes her feel. Price comparisons are prohibited as primary messaging.

### R-22: MVQUEEN Is For Women — Fully, Unapologetically
Every decision passes through: How does this make her feel? Does this honor her? Does this elevate her?

---

## SECTION 6 — AI AGENT AND OS RULES

### R-23: Every AI Output Must Know The Brand Before It Produces
MVQUEEN_CONTEXT.md is the minimum requirement for any AI session.

### R-24: The Brand Bible Is Always The Source Of Truth
In conflicts between files, the Brand Bible takes precedence.

### R-25: The OS Is A Living System — Files Are Versioned, Not Deleted
Archive before replacing. Nothing deleted without human confirmation.

---

## THE 10 BRAND COMMANDMENTS

1. The name is **MVQUEEN**. Always.
2. The sister brand is **Miss.Princess**. Always.
3. The color palette is warm, feminine, and luxury. Always.
4. The typography is editorial serif + clean sans-serif. Always.
5. The voice is warm, confident, and emotionally intelligent. Always.
6. She is always "she." The brand always speaks to her, not about her.
7. "Emotional" is used with precision, not as a filler word.
8. Every piece of content inspires, educates, or converts.
9. The brand never competes on price — only on feeling.
10. AI is a tool. The brand is the filter. Human review is always final.

---

*These rules protect what makes MVQUEEN MVQUEEN. When the rules are followed, every output sounds, looks, and feels like the brand. That consistency is the brand.*
