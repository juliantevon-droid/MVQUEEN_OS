# 👑 MVQUEEN — Brand Rules
### The DO / DON'T Reference

Every brand decision — visual, verbal, experiential — produces either something that builds MVQUEEN or something that quietly erodes it. These are the guardrails that keep the brand coherent at scale.

---

## SECTION 1 — VOICE & COPY RULES

✅ DO lead with feeling before function.
> ✅ "The kind of confidence you don't have to announce. Our [product] works quietly — and so do you."
> ❌ "Our hydrating serum contains 3 key ingredients for moisture retention."

✅ DO write in short, weighted sentences.
> ✅ "She already knew. This just made it visible."
> ❌ "This product is designed to help women feel more confident and beautiful in their everyday lives."

✅ DO use specificity over adjectives.
> ✅ "Matte finish. Weighted lid. The kind of packaging that makes throwing it away feel like a waste."
> ❌ "Beautiful, luxurious packaging you'll love."

✅ DO end on identity, not product.
> ✅ "This isn't about the product. It's about the woman who reaches for it every morning."
> ❌ "Shop now and experience the difference."

❌ DON'T use passive permission language.
> ❌ "Allow yourself to feel beautiful." ✅ "Feel beautiful. This is what that looks like."

❌ DON'T use exhausted empowerment language.
> ❌ "Slay in this boss babe collection." ✅ "For the woman who dresses like she's already decided."

❌ DON'T explain the emotion — show it.
> ❌ "This product creates an emotionally meaningful experience."
> ✅ "The kind of scent that makes you stand in the mirror a little longer."

❌ DON'T stack filler qualifiers. Remove: very, really, quite, just, truly, actually.

❌ DON'T use urgency or scarcity language.
> ❌ "Don't miss out! Last chance!" ✅ "Only a few remaining."

---

## SECTION 2 — VISUAL RULES

✅ DO use warm tones in every context. Ivory #FAF6F0, Blush #F2D4CC, Espresso #3B2314.
✅ DO let negative space work. One strong image. One line of copy.
✅ DO treat photography like emotional storytelling. Warm light, intimate, sensory texture.
✅ DO use the full type system. Cormorant display. Jost body. Always both.
✅ DO use Champagne Gold sparingly. One element per composition maximum.

❌ DON'T use pure white (#FFFFFF) or pure black (#000000).
❌ DON'T chase visual trends at the expense of identity.
❌ DON'T overcrowd product imagery. One product, beautifully lit, one prop max.
❌ DON'T use clip art, stock icons, or generic Canva elements.
❌ DON'T put Miss.Princess colors (Soft Lilac #E8DCF0, Petal Pink #F7E0DA) in MVQUEEN contexts.

---

## SECTION 3 — EXPERIENCE RULES

✅ DO treat every touchpoint as part of the product.
✅ DO make unboxing intentional. Ivory mailer, Dusty Rose tissue, Espresso stamp.
✅ DO write customer communications in brand voice.
> ✅ "Your order is on its way. We wrapped it with care."
> ❌ "Order #45621 has been processed and will ship within 3–5 business days."

❌ DON'T use shame or insecurity as purchase triggers.
> ❌ "Tired of dull skin? Finally fix it." ✅ "Skin that tells a story. This is the chapter where it glows."
❌ DON'T automate without brand voice review.
❌ DON'T treat low-ticket items as less important. Same care, every price point.
❌ DON'T use "luxury" without earning it. Show it first.

---

## SECTION 4 — BRAND CONSISTENCY TESTS

**Five-Second Test:** She should feel MVQUEEN within 5 seconds — from warmth, softness, confidence. Not from the logo.
**Mirror Test:** Does this look or sound like any other brand? If yes — rewrite.
**Brand Bible Test:** Does this contradict the Brand Bible? If yes — it doesn't happen.
**Forbidden Words Test:** Run every piece of copy through Forbidden_Words.md. No exceptions.
