## UNIVERSAL PRODUCT FAQs

### Q: What makes MVQUEEN products different from other brands?
**A:** MVQUEEN products are designed around how she feels when she uses them — not just what they do to her skin or hair. Every formula, texture, and scent is chosen to create a genuine ritual experience, not just a functional outcome. If she uses a MVQUEEN product and it doesn't feel elevated — it doesn't meet our standard.

---

### Q: Are MVQUEEN products cruelty-free?
**A:** Yes. All MVQUEEN products are cruelty-free. We do not test on animals and do not work with suppliers who do.

---

### Q: Are the products vegan?
**A:** Most are. Any product containing beeswax, honey, or silk proteins is labeled clearly. If she's looking for fully vegan options, the product description will confirm — or she can contact us directly.

---

### Q: Will these products work for my skin/hair type?
**A:** MVQUEEN formulates for real women across all skin tones, textures, and types. Product descriptions specify who each formula is designed for — including skin type guidance. When in doubt, the "who this is for" section of any product description is the place to start.

---

### Q: Do MVQUEEN products have strong fragrances?
**A:** Fragrance intensity varies by product and is specified in each description. Skincare products use soft, subtle scent profiles — never overpowering. Fragrance products (EDP, perfume oils, candles) are designed for scent to be the primary experience and are labeled with their scent family.

---

### Q: Are products safe for sensitive skin?
**A:** Beauty and skincare products are formulated to be gentle. For reactive or highly sensitive skin, patch testing on the inner wrist for 24 hours before full application is always recommended. Products specifically formulated for sensitive skin are labeled accordingly.

## SKINCARE FAQs

### Q: What order should I apply my MVQUEEN skincare products?
**A:** Thinnest to richest — always. The general order: mist or toner → serum → face oil → moisturizer. For the specific layering recommendation for her MVQUEEN products, the ritual guide in each product description has the exact sequence.

---

### Q: Can I use a serum and a face oil together?
**A:** Yes — and the results are worth it. Apply the serum first (it's water-based and penetrates deepest), then the face oil on top to seal everything in and add luminosity. Two drops of the Luminous Ritual Face Oil after the Soft Glow Serum is the pairing she'll keep coming back to.

---

### Q: How long before I see results from a serum?
**A:** Most women notice a difference within 7–14 days of consistent daily use. Some products show results faster — the product description specifies the typical timeline. Consistent application matters more than quantity.

---

### Q: Can I use MVQUEEN skincare products during pregnancy?
**A:** We recommend consulting with a healthcare provider before introducing new skincare during pregnancy — particularly for products containing retinol, vitamin A derivatives, or certain essential oils. Product formulations are available on request.

## BODY CARE FAQs

### Q: When is the best time to apply body butter or oil?
**A:** Right after showering, while skin is still slightly warm and damp. The warmth opens the pores and the residual moisture helps everything absorb deeper and faster. Two minutes post-shower, every time — that is the ritual.

---

### Q: Will body oil leave residue on my clothes?
**A:** MVQUEEN body oils are formulated to absorb fully within 60–90 seconds. Apply, wait for absorption, then dress. The Soft Ritual Body Oil in particular absorbs almost immediately — it is designed to be worn under clothing without transfer.

---

### Q: How often should I use the body scrub?
**A:** Once or twice per week is the sweet spot. More frequently can over-exfoliate. The Sugar Polish Scrub is most effective when followed immediately with body butter or oil — the freshly exfoliated skin absorbs everything deeper.

---

## HAIR CARE FAQs

### Q: How often should I use the hair mask?
**A:** Weekly for most hair types. For highly damaged, color-treated, or very dry hair: twice weekly until hair condition improves, then maintain at weekly. The Silk Restore Hair Mask can replace conditioner on treatment days.

---

### Q: Can I use hair oil on fine hair without it looking greasy?
**A:** Yes — the key is amount and placement. One drop maximum for fine hair. Apply only to mid-lengths and ends — never the root. The Soft Glow Hair Oil is particularly suitable for finer hair because of its ultra-lightweight formula.

---

### Q: Should I apply hair oil to wet or dry hair?
**A:** Both — for different purposes. On damp hair (before drying): adds shine and reduces frizz during styling. On dry hair (as a finish): adds gloss and controls flyaways. Start with less than she thinks she needs — she can always add, never take away.

---

## FRAGRANCE FAQs

### Q: How do I make my perfume last longer?
**A:** Apply to pulse points — wrists, neck, inner elbows, décolletage. Apply to moisturized skin (body lotion or oil beneath fragrance extends longevity significantly). Layer with the matching body mist for maximum wear. Do not rub wrists together after applying — this breaks down the molecules and shortens the scent.

---

### Q: What is the difference between Eau de Parfum and perfume oil?
**A:** Concentration and feel. EDP is alcohol-based — projects more immediately, ideal for daytime or events. Perfume oil has no alcohol — it sits closer to the skin, unfolds slower, and tends to feel more intimate. Many women wear perfume oil on pulse points and EDP on clothing for a full-day layered experience.

---

### Q: Are the fragrances safe for sensitive skin?
**A:** Perfume oils are generally more skin-friendly than EDPs because they are alcohol-free. All MVQUEEN fragrances are formulated without common irritants, but patch testing is recommended for anyone with fragrance sensitivity.
