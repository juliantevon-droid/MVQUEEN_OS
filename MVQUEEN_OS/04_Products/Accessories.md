# 💍 MVQUEEN — Accessories & Jewelry

---

## Category Overview

Accessories in MVQUEEN are not afterthoughts.
They are the details that complete the identity — the finishing touches that signal intention without announcing effort.

The right accessory in MVQUEEN's world does not dress up an outfit.
It confirms the woman wearing it.

---

## Accessories Philosophy

MVQUEEN accessories operate on one principle:
**Quiet luxury in the details.**

Not statement pieces that perform loudly.
Pieces that reward attention — that a woman notices on another woman and thinks: *she chose that intentionally.*

Every accessory in the MVQUEEN ecosystem should feel like it belongs to the brand world:
Warm. Feminine. Considered. Worth noticing.

## Jewelry Subcategory

### Fine-Look Jewelry (Accessible Luxury)
High-quality jewelry at accessible price points. Tarnish-resistant. Layerable. Wearable daily without thinking about it.

**Core pieces:**

- **Soft Chain Necklace** — Delicate gold or silver chain in two lengths: choker and collarbone. The necklace she puts on and forgets she's wearing — because it looks like it belongs there.

- **Hoop Earrings (Mini + Medium)** — The foundational earring. Gold-filled or silver-tone. Two sizes: one for everyday, one for evenings. The earring that works with every outfit in her rotation.

- **Pearl Drop Earrings** — Feminine. Soft. The earring she reaches for when she wants to look intentional without looking like she tried.

- **Layering Bracelet Set** — Thin, stackable. Mix of chain, delicate beads, and minimal charm. Worn together or alone. The set she never takes off.

- **Statement Ring** — One per collection. Designed to anchor a whole hand. Warm gold or silver, soft organic shape. The ring that gets noticed without demanding it.

### Hair Accessories
**Emotional promise:** The finishing touch that makes her feel finished.

**Core pieces:**

- **Silk Scrunchie Set** — In MVQUEEN core palette: champagne, blush, ivory, deep rose. Satin or silk finish. For her wrist as much as her hair.

- **Satin Hair Ribbon** — Tied in a bow on a ponytail or low bun. Worn casually or dressed up. The accessory that costs almost nothing and changes an entire look.

### Handbags & Bags
**Emotional promise:** The bag that completes the look without completing her budget.

**Core direction:**
Soft, structured, or somewhere elegant between the two.
Warm neutral palette: cream, champagne, cognac, blush, warm black.
Sizes that make sense for how she actually lives.

**Core pieces:**

- **Mini Shoulder Bag** — Small, structured, chain or thin strap. For evenings, brunch, or any day she doesn't want to carry the world with her.

- **Soft Tote** — The daily bag. Large enough for everything she actually needs. Soft leather or vegan alternative. No excessive hardware — clean lines, thoughtful proportions.

- **Quilted Crossbody** — The casual luxury bag. Light, hands-free, feminine. In blush, cream, or champagne. The bag that made her feel like a MVQUEEN customer before she knew what that meant.

- **Evening Clutch** — Minimal. Elegant. In satin, beaded, or metallic finish. For the events she gets dressed for — and the ones she doesn't need an excuse to attend.

---

### Scarves & Wraps
**Emotional promise:** Versatility that feels feminine — not practical.

**Core pieces:**

- **Silk Printed Square Scarf** — On her head, her bag handle, her wrist, or her neck. In soft MVQUEEN prints: floral, abstract, warm geometric. The one accessory with no wrong way to wear it.

- **Cashmere-Feel Wrap** — For the cooler seasons. Over shoulders, tied loosely, or worn as a light blanket on a flight. The kind of wrap she brings everywhere and feels under-dressed without.

## Accessories Copy Standard

Accessory copy centers on **what the piece does for the whole** — not its specifications.

**Wrong:** "A 14k gold-filled 16-inch delicate chain necklace with a lobster clasp."
**Right:** "The necklace that disappears into every outfit — except that it doesn't, because people notice it. Delicate gold chain, two lengths, wears daily."

Accessory copy answers: **what does this add to how she feels and how she looks?**
