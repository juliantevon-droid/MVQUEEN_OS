# 💰 MVQUEEN — Pricing Strategy

---

## Pricing Philosophy

MVQUEEN is positioned as accessible luxury.

That positioning has a specific meaning in pricing terms:
- Not mass market (pricing that signals disposability)
- Not aspirational luxury (pricing that creates exclusion)
- The emotional middle ground — prices that feel considered, that signal real quality, and that do not require the customer to feel financially stressed to access the brand

The goal is for every woman in MVQUEEN's target audience to be able to buy something she genuinely wants without a purchase creating anxiety.

## Pricing Psychology

### Charm Pricing (modified for luxury)
Standard charm pricing ends in .99 — MVQUEEN does not use this.
It signals discount-brand psychology.

MVQUEEN pricing ends in:
- **.00** — for round, clean pricing on core products
- **.50** — occasionally, for a slight softness
- Never **.99**, **.97**, or **.95**

**Example:**
Wrong: $24.99
Right: $25.00 or $24.50

### Price Anchoring
Every collection page features products across a price range.
The highest-priced item anchors perception — making mid-range items feel accessible by comparison.

Always ensure:
- Entry product at accessible price (under $25)
- Core product at mid range ($28–$48)
- Premium product or kit at higher price ($52+)

### Bundle Pricing
Kits and sets are priced to deliver perceived savings of 15–20% versus buying individually.
The saving is real — not manufactured. She can verify it. That builds trust.

## Sale & Discount Policy

MVQUEEN uses promotional pricing strategically — not reactively.

**Approved promotional contexts:**
- Seasonal collections transitioning (end of season edit)
- New customer welcome offer (10–15% off first order)
- Loyalty tier rewards (see `25_Retention_And_Community`)
- Birthday offers to loyalty members
- Bundling discounts (kit pricing)

**Never used:**
- Sitewide flash sales with no context
- Artificial urgency discounts ("Today only!")
- Discounting hero products outside of structured loyalty rewards
- Price reductions that undermine the accessible luxury positioning

Discounting a luxury brand carelessly damages the emotional positioning that justifies the price in the first place.

---

## Compare-At Pricing (Shopify)

Compare-at pricing shows a product's "original" price versus current price.
MVQUEEN uses this only for:
- End-of-season clearance
- Officially communicated promotional events
- Bundle savings (comparing bundle price to individual item total)

Never used to create manufactured urgency on standard-priced products.
