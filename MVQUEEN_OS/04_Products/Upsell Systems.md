## Upselling in a Luxury Context

Upselling in MVQUEEN is never pushy.
It is guidance — helping her make the better choice for her ritual, her results, and her value.

The framing is always: **"Here is the version that gives her more."**
Never: **"Upgrade! More expensive = better."**

A well-executed upsell should make her feel like she discovered a smarter option — not like she was sold up.

---

## Core Upsell Opportunities

### Size Upsells
**Context:** She is buying a product she will use regularly.
**The offer:** A larger size at a better price-per-ml.

**Formula:**
> "She'll reach for this every morning. The 100ml lasts twice as long — at 30% better value."

**Products with size upsell:**
- Body oils (50ml → 100ml)
- Body butters (150ml → 250ml)
- Shampoo + Conditioner (200ml → 300ml)
- Face mists (100ml → 150ml)

**Price difference rule:** The larger size must deliver a genuine per-unit saving of minimum 15%. Never manufacture a false saving.

---

### Kit Upsells
**Context:** She is buying a single product that belongs to a ritual.
**The offer:** The full ritual kit at a bundle saving.

**Formula:**
> "The Morning Ritual Starter Set includes the serum, the face oil, and the mist — saving her $18 versus buying separately."

**Key kits with upsell logic:**
- Morning Ritual Starter Set (serum + face oil + mist)
- Luminous Skin Duo (face oil + glow serum)
- Velvet Body Ritual Set (body butter + body oil + scrub)
- Hair Restore Kit (mask + oil + shampoo)

**Placement:** Show kit upsell when individual kit component is in cart.

---

### Refill / Replenishment Upsells
**Context:** She is buying a product she has bought before or will buy again.
**The offer:** Subscribe or buy double now at a saving.

*(Future implementation — requires subscription app)*

**Formula:**
> "She buys this every 6 weeks. Subscribe and save 10% — cancel any time."

## Upsell Placement

### Pre-Cart (Product Page)
Below the Add to Cart button — above the cross-sell section.
One upsell option only.
Labeled: **"The better value"** or **"She'll want the full set"**

### In-Cart
Shown when a single product from a kit is in cart.
One offer — the kit that contains it.
Labeled: **"Complete the set and save"**

### Post-Purchase (Email)
Triggered 7-14 days after delivery for products with high repeat purchase.
Framed as replenishment, not upsell.
Labeled: **"Running low? She usually reorders around now."**

---

## Upsell Copy Rules

1. Always show the saving in dollar or percentage — not implied
2. Never pressure ("upgrade before it's too late")
3. Frame in her ritual — why does the bigger/better version serve her better?
4. One upsell per placement — never more than one competing offer
5. The copy explains the value in one sentence
