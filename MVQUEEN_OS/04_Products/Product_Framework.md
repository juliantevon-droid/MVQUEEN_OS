# 🏗️ MVQUEEN — Product Framework

---

## What This Document Is

The Product Framework defines how every MVQUEEN product is conceived, developed, positioned, and launched — from initial concept to live listing.

It is the structural architecture that ensures every product that enters the MVQUEEN ecosystem meets the same emotional, aesthetic, and commercial standard.

Nothing launches without passing through this framework.

---

## The MVQUEEN Product Philosophy

MVQUEEN products are not designed to fill a catalog.
They are designed to fill an emotional gap.

Before any product is developed, one question must be answered:
**What does she feel when she uses this — and is that feeling something she cannot get from anything else in this collection?**

If the answer is unclear — the product is not ready to develop.
If the answer is specific, emotionally resonant, and distinct — development begins.

## Product Development Stages

### STAGE 01 — The Emotional Brief

Before sourcing, pricing, or naming — define the emotional truth of this product.

Every product starts with an Emotional Brief that answers:

```
EMOTIONAL PROMISE:    What feeling does this product create?
PERSONA FIT:          Which MVQUEEN customer avatar is this for?
RITUAL PLACEMENT:     Where does this live in her day/week/routine?
CATEGORY:             Which of the 7 product categories does this belong to?
DIFFERENTIATION:      How is this distinct from everything else in the collection?
IDENTITY STATEMENT:   Complete this: "This product is for the woman who ___"
```

No product brief moves to Stage 02 without a complete, honest answer to all six.

---

### STAGE 02 — Product Intelligence

Research, source, and validate before committing.

**Supplier research:**
- Minimum 3 supplier quotes per product
- Sample before ordering any volume
- Evaluate against: quality standard, MOQ viability, lead time, packaging options

**Competitive intelligence:**
- How do competitors in this category present this product?
- Where is the emotional gap MVQUEEN can fill?
- What price point positions this correctly within accessible luxury?

**Customer validation:**
- Does this product address a real unmet desire from the personas?
- Is there search demand (keyword research via `Product SEO.md`)?
- Is there social proof of interest (trending on TikTok, Pinterest saves)?

---

**Product Architecture document includes:**

---

### STAGE 04 — Copy & Presentation

With the product architecture complete — write the copy.

Every MVQUEEN product requires:

**1. Product Name** — from `Product_Naming_System.md`
**2. Short Description** — 2-3 sentences, emotion-first (from `Product_Description_Voice.md`)
**3. Long Description** — 4-6 paragraphs following the description architecture
**4. Bullet Points** — 5 benefit statements, experience-language format
**5. SEO Title** — 50-60 characters, keyword-informed
**6. SEO Meta Description** — 150-160 characters, emotional + searchable
**7. Shopify Tags** — minimum 8, from approved tag taxonomy

All copy checked against `Forbidden_Words.md` before final.

---

### STAGE 05 — Photography & Visual Assets

Every product requires:

| Shot Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Clean product shot (white/neutral background) | Shopify thumbnail |
| Lifestyle shot (in her world, warm light) | Product page hero |
| Texture/detail shot (macro) | Secondary product image |
| In-use shot (hands, skin, application) | Social and email |
| Flatlay (with complementary products) | Collection and social |

Photography standards: `Product Photography Standards.md`
Every product image follows the warm, soft, feminine visual standard. No exceptions.

---

### STAGE 06 — Commercial Setup

**Shopify setup checklist:**
- [ ] Product title matches Product Architecture doc
- [ ] All copy fields populated (title, description, SEO fields)
- [ ] All variants created and priced
- [ ] Inventory tracking enabled
- [ ] Collections assigned
- [ ] Tags applied
- [ ] Cross-sell and upsell products linked
- [ ] Product active / draft status confirmed

---

### STAGE 07 — Launch

Every product launch — even a single SKU — follows the Launch SOP in `39_SOP_Library`.

Minimum launch requirements:
- One email to full list or relevant segment
- Two social posts (one product-focused, one lifestyle/ritual)
- Paid amplification (if budget allocated)
- SMS to VIP segment

No product goes live and goes silent. Every launch has a communication plan.

## Product Hierarchy in the Collection

```
MVQUEEN ECOSYSTEM
    ↓
CATEGORY (e.g., Beauty & Glow Essentials)
    ↓
COLLECTION (e.g., Luminous Ritual Collection)
    ↓
HERO PRODUCT (the anchor — most emotionally and commercially significant)
    ↓
SUPPORTING PRODUCTS (complementary, extend the ritual)
    ↓
ADD-ONS (accessories, tools, bundles)
```

Every collection needs one hero. The hero carries the emotional story. Supporting products build the ecosystem around it.

---

## Product Quality Standards

A MVQUEEN product earns its place by meeting all five:

1. **Emotional standard** — Creates the feeling it promises
2. **Visual standard** — Photographs beautifully in warm, feminine light
3. **Sensory standard** — Feels premium to touch, open, use
4. **Voice standard** — Can be described in MVQUEEN voice without strain
5. **Value standard** — Delivers genuine value at its price point — accessible luxury, not cheap luxury
