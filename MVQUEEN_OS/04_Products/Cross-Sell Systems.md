# 🔁 MVQUEEN — Cross-Sell Systems

---

## What Cross-Selling Does for MVQUEEN

Cross-selling increases average order value without pressure.
Done well — it feels like curation, not commerce.

The woman who buys the Soft Ritual Body Oil should see the Velvet Body Butter as the natural companion.
Not because a popup told her to buy more — but because the pairing makes sense for her ritual.

MVQUEEN cross-sells by building ritual ecosystems, not product adjacencies.

---

## The Cross-Sell Philosophy

**Wrong approach:** "Customers also bought..."
**MVQUEEN approach:** "Complete the ritual."

Every cross-sell recommendation should feel like it was made by someone who understands her routine — not by an algorithm that noticed purchase co-occurrence.

The framing: **"This belongs with it."**
Not: **"Here's more you can buy."**

## Cross-Sell Architecture by Category

### Skincare Cross-Sells

**If she buys a serum →** suggest:
1. The face oil that layers with it (Luminous Ritual Face Oil)
2. The moisturizer that seals it in (Velvet Face Cream)
3. The mist that preps the skin before application (Micellar Rose Mist)

*Ritual framing:* "The complete luminous morning ritual."

**If she buys a cleanser →** suggest:
1. The exfoliant for weekly ritual (Soft Polish Exfoliant)
2. The mask that follows cleansing (Honey Glow Mask)
3. The serum to apply post-cleanse (Soft Glow Daily Serum)

*Ritual framing:* "Start her ritual right — complete the cleanse."

**If she buys a mask →** suggest:
1. The serum applied after the mask (Overnight Renewal Serum)
2. The moisturizer that seals the treatment
3. The sheet mask companion for different skin needs

## Cross-Sell Placement in Shopify

### Product Page — "Complete the Ritual" Section
- Placed below the fold on product page
- 3 products maximum — never more
- Labeled: **"Complete the ritual"** or **"She also reaches for"**
- All 3 products are manually curated per product — not auto-generated

### Cart — "One more thing"
- 1 product shown in cart drawer
- Selected based on highest emotional/ritual alignment with cart contents
- Labeled: **"Before you go —"**
- Never show the same category already in cart

### Post-Purchase Email — "Built for what she just chose"
- Triggered 3 days post-delivery
- 2 product recommendations
- Framed as ritual completion, not upsell
- Labeled: **"Now that she has [product name]..."**

## Cross-Sell Copy Standard

Every cross-sell module needs a line of copy — never just product tiles.

**Wrong:** [Product image] [Price] [Add to cart]
**Right:**
> "The Sugar Polish prepares her skin for everything that follows. Used the week before — her body butter absorbs deeper, stays longer."

One sentence. Ritual framing. Makes the connection clear without pressure.

## Cross-Sell Performance Tracking

Track monthly:
- Click-through rate by cross-sell placement
- Add-to-cart rate from cross-sell
- Revenue attributed to cross-sell recommendations
- Which pairings perform highest → promote to hero pairings

Update cross-sell assignments quarterly based on performance data.
