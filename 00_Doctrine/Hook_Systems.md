# MVQUEEN — Hook Systems (12 Formulas)

## Formula 01 — Identity Confirmation
Structure: State who she already is. Make her feel seen.
Formula: For the woman who ___.

MVQUEEN: For the woman who already knows what she's worth. / For the woman who doesn't need to announce herself.
Miss. Princess: For the girl who's figuring it out — beautifully. / For the girl who just wants to feel good today. ✦

## Formula 02 — The Observation
Structure: Name something true about her experience with no product mention.
Formula: You don't [problem stated gently]. You just haven't found [implied solution] yet.

MVQUEEN: She doesn't overthink her outfit. She just needs pieces that work.
Miss. Princess: You don't need more products. You need the right ones. ✦

## Formula 03 — The Contrast
Structure: Set up what luxury usually is vs. what MVQUEEN does differently.
Formula: Most [category] brands ___. We ___.

Miss. Princess: Most beauty brands are made for someone else. Miss. Princess was made for you. ✦
