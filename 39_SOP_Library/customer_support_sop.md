# SOP: Customer Support & Emotional Connection
## MVQUEEN_OS / 39_SOP_Library

---

## 1. Purpose
Governs all customer interactions to ensure they feel like an extension of the MVQueen brand—warm, intentional, and restorative.

---

## 2. Voice Guidelines
*   **Never** use generic corporate language (e.g., "Your call is important to us").
*   **Always** speak to the woman, not the ticket number.
*   **Tone:** Empathetic, calm, and solution-oriented.

---

## 3. Common Scenarios

### Scenario A: Refund Request
*   **Response:** "We are sorry to hear that your piece didn't bring the softness you expected. We want you to feel entirely at home with MVQueen."
*   **Action:** Process refund immediately according to the `Refund_SOP.md`.

### Scenario B: Product Inquiry
*   **Response:** Use sensory details from the `Product_Catalog.md` to describe the feel and fit.
*   **Action:** Provide a direct link to the product.

---

## 4. Response Cadence
*   **Emails:** Within 12 hours.
*   **Social Media DMs:** Within 4 hours during business hours.
