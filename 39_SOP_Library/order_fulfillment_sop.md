# SOP: Order Fulfillment & Luxury Experience
## MVQUEEN_OS / 39_SOP_Library

---

## 1. Purpose
To ensure every MVQueen order is fulfilled with the precision and emotional warmth expected of a luxury brand.

---

## 2. Fulfillment Rituals
1.  **Notification:** Review new orders in Shopify Admin every 4 hours.
2.  **Quality Control:** Inspect every item for defects before packaging.
3.  **The Unboxing Ritual:**
    *   Use branded tissue paper (softness).
    *   Include a handwritten thank-you note or a high-quality brand card.
    *   Ensure the packaging smells like the signature MVQueen fragrance (if applicable).
4.  **Labeling:** Use clean, centered shipping labels.

---

## 3. Shipping Protocols
*   **Standard:** 3-5 business days.
*   **Express:** 1-2 business days.
*   **Tracking:** Ensure tracking numbers are automatically sent to the customer via Shopify.

---

## 4. Crisis Management
*   **Out of Stock:** Contact the customer within 12 hours with an emotional apology and an upgrade/refund offer.
*   **Delayed Shipping:** Proactively notify the customer and provide a "Softness Credit" (discount code) for future use.
