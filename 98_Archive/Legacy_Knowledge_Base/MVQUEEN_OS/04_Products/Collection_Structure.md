# 💎 MVQUEEN — Collection Structure

---

## Collections Are Worlds, Not Groupings

A collection in MVQUEEN is not a folder of products with a shared label.
It is an emotionally cohesive world — a curated moment in time with a distinct atmosphere, story, and identity that makes her feel she is entering something intentional.

When she enters a collection, she should feel the collection before she sees the products.

That feeling is what drives her to explore. And then to buy.

---

## Collection Architecture

```
MVQUEEN BRAND WORLD
    ↓
CORE COLLECTIONS (permanent — always live)
    ↓
SEASONAL COLLECTIONS (quarterly — tied to emotional timing)
    ↓
LIMITED EDITION DROPS (moment-based — scarcity and exclusivity)
    ↓
PERSONA COLLECTIONS (avatar-specific — deeply targeted)
    ↓
COLLABORATION COLLECTIONS (brand partnerships — future phase)
```

---

## TIER 01 — Core Collections (Always Live)

These are the permanent anchors of the MVQUEEN catalog. They do not retire with the season. They evolve as the brand grows.

### The Luminous Collection
**Emotional world:** Glow. Radiance. Skin that catches light and holds it.
**Products:** Face oils, serums, highlighters, glow-enhancing body care
**Her identity:** The woman whose skin announces her before she speaks
**Visual direction:** Warm gold light, dewy skin, champagne surfaces

### The Velvet Collection
**Emotional world:** Softness. Depth. The kind of richness that feels like being wrapped in something expensive.
**Products:** Body butters, rich creams, hair masks, deep moisture formulas
**Her identity:** The woman who takes care of herself with seriousness and intention
**Visual direction:** Deep blush, warm cocoa tones, velvet and linen textures

### The Ritual Collection
**Emotional world:** Devotion. The sacred five minutes she protects from the world.
**Products:** Ritual kits, cleansing balms, face mists, morning and evening essentials
**Her identity:** The woman who built her routine around herself — not around convenience
**Visual direction:** Clean marble surfaces, soft morning light, hands in motion

### The Signature Fragrance Collection
**Emotional world:** Identity. Mood. The atmosphere she carries with her.
**Products:** Eau de Parfum, perfume oils, body mists, candles
**Her identity:** The woman whose scent becomes how people remember her
**Visual direction:** Amber and warm glass, candlelight, soft blur

## TIER 02 — Seasonal Collections (Quarterly)

Released in alignment with emotional and seasonal shifts. Named for feeling — not for season alone.

**Q1 — Renewal (January–March)**
Energy: Fresh start without pressure. Intentional rebuilding.
Theme: Detox rituals, skin restoration, the new routine she keeps.
Color direction: Soft whites, pale blush, barely-there champagne.

**Q2 — Bloom (April–June)**
Energy: Opening. Softness expanding. The warmth she's been waiting for.
Theme: Glow, light textures, fresh fragrance, effortless beauty.
Color direction: Petal pink, warm ivory, soft gold.

**Q3 — Golden (July–September)**
Energy: Warmth at its peak. The woman at her most radiant.
Theme: Body care, sun-kissed glow, warm fragrance, the ease of summer turning golden.
Color direction: Honey, amber, bronzed neutrals.

**Q4 — Velvet Season (October–December)**
Energy: Depth, warmth, protection. The season she turns inward.
Theme: Rich textures, warm scents, gift sets, the ritual that sustains her through winter.
Color direction: Deep rose, champagne gold, dark warm neutrals.

---

## TIER 03 — Limited Edition Drops

Released 4-6 times per year. Always named. Always scarce. Always emotionally charged.

**Drop format:**
- Maximum 5 products per drop
- Named collection title (not "Limited Edition Drop #3")
- Visual identity distinct from core — but still unmistakably MVQUEEN
- Clear availability window communicated upfront
- No restocks unless explicitly communicated as "one-time restock"

**Drop naming examples:**
- The Midnight Ritual Drop
- The Soft Power Edit
- The Queen's Birthday Collection
- The Golden Hour Drop

## TIER 04 — Persona Collections

Deep dives built around one specific MVQUEEN customer persona. Everything — product selection, copy, imagery, naming — is calibrated to that woman specifically.

**Soft Glam Queen Edit** — warmth, glow, sensory richness
**The Ambitious Edit** — intentional, precise, power-dressing beauty
**The Romanticizer's Collection** — fragrance-led, atmospheric, poetic
**The Ritual Devotee** — full ritual systems, layered routines, deep care

## Collection Page Architecture

Every collection page on Shopify follows this structure:

**1. Collection Hero**
Full-width image. Atmospheric. Emotional. No product visible in the hero shot.
Collection name in serif typography. Tagline or atmospheric sentence beneath.

**2. Collection Story (100–150 words)**
Written in MVQUEEN voice. Sets the emotional world of the collection.
She should feel something before she sees a product.

**3. Hero Product Feature**
The anchor product of the collection. Featured with full lifestyle imagery.
Short emotional description — 2-3 sentences.

**4. Product Grid**
Clean grid. Consistent imagery. Warm tones throughout.
Sorted: hero product first, then supporting products, then add-ons.

**5. Collection CTA**
"Discover the full ritual" or "Enter the collection" — never "Shop now"
