## Category Overview

Hair care in MVQUEEN is not maintenance.
It is restoration — the practice of returning her hair to the version it is when it is properly taken care of.

For many women, their hair is deeply tied to their identity and confidence.
MVQUEEN's hair care products honor that relationship.

Every product in this category delivers one of three core feelings:
**Restored** (repaired, nourished, stronger) · **Free** (soft, defined, moveable) · **Confident** (the hair she has when she's done it right)

---

## Hair Care Subcategories

### Hair Masks & Deep Treatments
**Emotional promise:** The weekly ritual that makes the rest of the week possible.

**Core products:**
- **Silk Restore Hair Mask** — Deep conditioning treatment for all hair types and textures. Applied post-shampoo, left for 10-20 minutes. The difference is not subtle — she'll feel it on the first rinse. Soft, defined, and manageable in a way that makes her want to touch it.
- **Moisture Revival Treatment** — For hair that has been through something — heat, color, weather, neglect. Not a punishment for bad habits. An act of care. Applied weekly to bring moisture back to where it belongs.
- **Protein Strength Treatment** — For breakage-prone or chemically processed hair. Rebuilds from the inside. Used monthly as a strengthening ritual — the product that changes the conversation between her and her hair.

---

### Hair Oils & Serums
**Emotional promise:** The finishing touch that changes how everything looks.

**Core products:**
- **Soft Glow Hair Oil** — Lightweight, non-greasy finishing oil. A few drops through mid-lengths and ends. Adds shine without weight — the difference between hair that looks styled and hair that looks like hers.
- **Scalp Nourishment Oil** — For the root of everything. Massaged into the scalp before washing or overnight. When her scalp is healthy, her hair shows it. This is the product that works furthest upstream.
- **Frizz Control Serum** — Applied to damp or dry hair. Seals the cuticle, eliminates frizz, and leaves softness without stiffness. For the mornings when the humidity has opinions and she has none of the time.

---

### Shampoo & Conditioner
**Emotional promise:** A clean that prepares the hair — not strips it.

**Core products:**
- **Moisture Balance Shampoo** — Cleanses without compromising. For weekly or bi-weekly use. The shampoo that doesn't leave her hair feeling like it needs to recover from being washed.
- **Hydration Conditioner** — Daily or post-shampoo. Detangles, softens, and seals. Applied mid-length to ends, rinsed in cool water. Her hair's daily drink of water.
- **Clarifying Shampoo** — Monthly use. Removes buildup from products, hard water, and the environment. The reset she gives her hair when it starts feeling heavy or dull. Used before a deep treatment for maximum results.

---

### Styling & Finishing
**Emotional promise:** Hair that does what she needs — and stays that way.

**Core products:**
- **Defining Curl Cream** — For textured, wavy, or curly hair. Enhances definition without crunch. Applied to wet hair, scrunched in gently. Her curls, on the best day they have — every day.
- **Thermal Protection Spray** — Applied before any heat styling. Protects without weighing down. The product she uses even when she's in a rush — because her hair's health is not negotiable.
- **Shine Mist** — A light finishing mist for any hair type. Adds instant luminosity and refreshes second-day hair. The bottle she keeps on her nightstand for the morning after.

## Hair Care by Hair Type

### Fine & Straight
Priorities: volume without weight, shine without greasiness, longevity
Recommended: Moisture Balance Shampoo, Hydration Conditioner, Soft Glow Hair Oil (1-2 drops), Shine Mist

### Wavy & Medium Texture
Priorities: definition without stiffness, frizz control, hydration
Recommended: Moisture Balance Shampoo, Silk Restore Mask (weekly), Frizz Control Serum, Defining Curl Cream

### Curly & Coily
Priorities: moisture retention, curl definition, strength, reduced breakage
Recommended: Co-wash or gentle shampoo, Moisture Revival Treatment (weekly), Scalp Nourishment Oil, Defining Curl Cream, Protein Strength Treatment (monthly)

### Color-Treated
Priorities: color preservation, moisture replacement, strength
Recommended: Color-safe shampoo, Silk Restore Mask (2x weekly), Protein Strength Treatment (monthly), Thermal Protection on all heat styling

## Hair Care Ritual Frameworks

### The Weekly Ritual
**Sunday evening (or whenever she has time):**
1. Scalp Nourishment Oil — massaged in 30 minutes before washing
2. Moisture Balance Shampoo — thorough cleanse
3. Silk Restore Mask — 15-20 minutes, deep condition
4. Cool water rinse — seals the cuticle
5. Soft Glow Hair Oil — 2-3 drops on damp ends
6. Air dry or style with Thermal Protection

### The Daily Ritual
1. Moisture spray or Shine Mist to refresh
2. Frizz Control Serum on any escaping pieces
3. Done — her hair on its best daily behavior

## Product Voice Standard for Hair Care

Hair copy centers one thing above all: **the relationship between her and her hair.**

Not: "A deep conditioning mask with keratin and argan oil."
Yes: "The mask that changes how her hair behaves for the rest of the week — softer, more manageable, more like itself."

Hair care copy always names:
1. The hair concern she has (without judgment)
2. What the product does to address it
3. The sensory experience of using it
4. The result she feels and sees
5. When and how it fits in her ritual
