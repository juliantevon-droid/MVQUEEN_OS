## Product SEO Philosophy

Every product page is a search landing page.
When a woman searches for "luxury body butter" or "glow serum for dry skin" — MVQUEEN's product pages should meet her there.

Product SEO in MVQUEEN is not separate from product copy.
It is embedded in it — language that is simultaneously emotionally resonant and search-optimized.

See also: `06_Tone_And_Voice/SEO Voice.md` for the full SEO voice framework.

## Product Page SEO Structure

Every MVQUEEN product page requires:

### Title Tag (50-60 characters)
`[Product Name] — [Primary keyword] | MVQUEEN`

Examples:
- `Velvet Body Butter — Luxury Moisturizing Body Cream | MVQUEEN`
- `Luminous Face Oil — Glow Serum for Dry Skin | MVQUEEN`
- `Silk Restore Hair Mask — Deep Conditioning Treatment | MVQUEEN`

### Meta Description (150-160 characters)
`[Emotional opener] [what it does + keyword] [persona signal or CTA]`

Examples:
- `Rich body butter that absorbs in seconds and holds moisture for hours. Formulated for women who want their skin to feel taken care of — all day.`
- `A lightweight face oil that creates luminous, settled skin in two drops. Her morning ritual — simplified. Free shipping on orders over $50.`

### H1 (Product Title on Page)
Same as Shopify product title — no duplication needed.

### Product Description (SEO integration)
- First 150 words include primary keyword naturally
- H2 sub-heading within description if long-form (optional, product-dependent)
- No keyword stuffing — maximum 2 exact-match mentions per page
- Synonyms and related terms used throughout naturally

### Image Alt Text
Every product image requires alt text:
- Format: `[Brand] [Product Name] [Description for screen reader / SEO]`
- Example: `MVQUEEN Velvet Body Butter jar on marble surface with warm lighting`
- Never: `IMG_4532.jpg` or blank alt text

## Internal Linking Strategy

Every product page links to:
1. Its primary collection page
2. 1-2 cross-sell products (natural anchor text)
3. 1 relevant blog post (where live)

This distributes link authority through the catalog and creates pathways for discovery.

---

## Schema Markup (Shopify)

Ensure these schema elements are active on every product page:
- Product schema (name, price, availability, brand)
- Review/rating schema (when reviews are present)
- Breadcrumb schema (collection → product)

These improve search appearance — rich snippets, star ratings in SERPs — without requiring additional content effort.
