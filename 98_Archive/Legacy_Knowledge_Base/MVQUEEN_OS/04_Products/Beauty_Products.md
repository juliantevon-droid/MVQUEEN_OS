# 🌸 MVQUEEN — Beauty & Skincare Products

---

## Category Overview

Beauty and skincare is the emotional core of MVQUEEN's product ecosystem.
It is where she meets the brand most intimately — touching her skin, breathing the scent, feeling the texture every single morning.

Every beauty product in this category exists to deliver one of three core feelings:
**Luminous** (glow, radiance, light) · **Restored** (nourishment, repair, care) · **Settled** (calm, grounded, tended to)

## Skincare Subcategories

### Face Serums & Oils
**Emotional promise:** The glow she gets asked about. Skin that looks like it has been taken care of.

**Core products:**
- **Luminous Ritual Face Oil** — Lightweight facial oil with a warm golden finish. 2-3 drops morning or evening. For the woman whose skin deserves more than just hydration — it deserves atmosphere.
- **Soft Glow Serum** — A water-light serum formulated for daily brightness. Sinks in fast. Works quietly. The kind of product she doesn't realize is doing everything until she skips it once.
- **Overnight Renewal Serum** — Her skin's second shift. Applied before sleep, it works while she rests. She wakes up different — not dramatically, just noticeably more like herself.

**Copy direction:** Lead with the morning moment. Describe the light quality of her skin. Name what changes subtly — not what transforms dramatically.

## Product Voice Standard for Beauty

Every beauty product description follows the architecture in `Product_Description_Voice.md`.

The non-negotiables for beauty copy:
1. **Texture before ingredients** — describe how it feels, not what's in it
2. **Ritual placement** — where in her day does this live?
3. **Emotional result** — how does she feel after, not just how her skin looks
4. **Sensory specificity** — scent, weight, absorption time, finish
5. **Identity close** — who is she when she uses this product?
