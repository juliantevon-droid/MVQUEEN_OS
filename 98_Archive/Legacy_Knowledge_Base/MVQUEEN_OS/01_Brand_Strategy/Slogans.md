# 👑 MVQUEEN — Slogans & Campaign Lines

---

## Slogans vs. Taglines

The tagline is permanent. It lives on the brand forever.
Slogans are campaign-specific — they carry a specific emotional direction for a defined moment, season, or collection.

A slogan lives for a campaign.
The tagline lives for the brand.

Both must sound like MVQUEEN.

## How to Use the Slogan Bank

**One per campaign** — never combine slogans in the same execution.

**Select by emotional direction:**

| Campaign Goal | Best Slogan Category |
|--------------|---------------------|
| New launch | Product Launch Slogans |
| Brand awareness | Confidence & Identity |
| Community building | Softness & Femininity |
| Seasonal campaign | Seasonal Slogans |
| Self-care product | Ritual & Self-Care |
| Premium positioning | Luxury & Elevation |

**Pairing with tagline:**
Slogans support the tagline — they do not replace it.
The tagline anchors the brand. The slogan carries the campaign.

---

## Writing New Slogans

When a campaign moment requires a new slogan:

**Rules:**
1. Under 10 words — preferably under 7
2. Passes the read-aloud test — sounds like a real woman said it
3. Centers her, not the product
4. No exclamation marks
5. Emotionally specific — not broadly aspirational
6. Could only have been written by MVQUEEN — not any other brand

**Test:** Read it next to "She always knew." Does it belong in the same family?
If yes — it's MVQUEEN. If not — rewrite.
