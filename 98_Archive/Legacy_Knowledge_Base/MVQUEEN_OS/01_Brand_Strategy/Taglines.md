# 👑 MVQUEEN — Taglines

---

## Tagline Philosophy

A tagline is the shortest possible version of the brand truth.
It must work in three places simultaneously:
- Spoken aloud — does it sound like MVQUEEN?
- Seen on packaging — does it feel like luxury?
- Read on a screen — does it land emotionally?

MVQUEEN taglines are never clever for cleverness' sake.
They are emotionally precise — the fewest words that carry the most feeling.

---

## Primary Brand Tagline

### **"Most Valuable. Most Her."**

**Why this works:**
- Anchors to the brand name's meaning (Most Valuable Queen)
- Centers the woman, not the product
- "Most Her" does something no competitor says — it defines luxury as self-alignment, not status
- Works across every category: fashion, beauty, fragrance, skincare
- Timeless — not trend-dependent

## Tagline Usage Rules

1. **Never modify the primary tagline** — no creative variations in official brand applications
2. **Secondary taglines rotate** — choose the one most aligned with the specific context
3. **One tagline per execution** — never stack them
4. **Taglines are not captions** — do not use as social media captions without supporting copy
5. **Typography treatment** — taglines set in serif italic for elegance when displayed as graphic type

---

## What MVQUEEN Taglines Are Never

| Avoid | Why |
|-------|-----|
| "Feel your best" | Generic — every brand says this |
| "Luxury for less" | Discount positioning — undermines emotional luxury |
| "Be you" | Empty — says nothing specific |
| "Elevate yourself" | Self-improvement framing — MVQUEEN doesn't fix her, it recognizes her |
| Anything with an exclamation mark | Against voice standard |
| Rhyming slogans | Sounds playful, not premium |
