# 👑 MVQUEEN PRODUCT DESCRIPTION VOICE
*The complete system for writing product descriptions that feel like MVQUEEN*

---

## PURPOSE

MVQUEEN product descriptions lead with feeling. By the time she's finished reading, she doesn't just know what the product is — she knows how it will make her feel. And that is why she buys.

---

## THE 5-PART FORMULA

**PART 1: THE EMOTIONAL HOOK**
One sentence. One feeling. Opens by speaking directly to her desire, her identity, or her aspirational self — not the product.

**PART 2: THE SENSORY STORY**
2–4 sentences. How it looks, feels, smells, moves, wears. She imagines herself with it.

**PART 3: THE FUNCTIONAL TRUTH**
2–3 bullets. Real features: ingredients, materials, dimensions, sizes, finish. Precise, never clinical.

**PART 4: THE IDENTITY STAMP**
One sentence. She sees herself in this product.

**PART 5: THE INVITATION**
One line. Soft CTA energy — an invitation, not a command.

## TEMPLATES BY CATEGORY

### FRAGRANCE TEMPLATE
Hook: [Emotional desire / identity statement about scent and presence]
Sensory Story: [Notes described in feelings and atmospheres, not just ingredients]
Functional Truth: Top notes / Heart notes / Base notes / Size / Concentration / Longevity
Identity Stamp: [Who this fragrance is for — through personality or lifestyle]
Invitation: [One soft closing line]

**EXAMPLE:**
> Some scents announce you. This one becomes you.
> A fragrance for the woman who enters a room quietly and leaves an impression that stays. Opens with fresh bergamot and soft peony. Deepens into rose and jasmine. Settles into warm amber, sandalwood, and vanilla — intimate, lingering, undeniably hers.
> Top: Bergamot, Peony · Heart: Rose, Jasmine · Base: Amber, Sandalwood, Vanilla · 50ml/100ml · EDP · 6–8 hours
> For the woman who wants to be remembered — without saying a word.
> *Wear it like you already know.*

---

### SKINCARE TEMPLATE
Hook: [How her skin feels vs. how she wants it to feel]
Sensory Story: [Texture, application, visible results, ritual moment]
Functional Truth: Key ingredients / Skin type / When to use / How to use / Size
Identity Stamp: [She who uses this — through a self-care lens]
Invitation: [Soft closing line]

**EXAMPLE:**
> She started using this serum and her skin started to glow. Then she started to glow.
> A weightless, water-silk formula that absorbs in seconds. In the morning, her skin looks rested, luminous, plumped from the inside.
> Hyaluronic Acid, Vitamin C, Niacinamide · All skin types · AM and PM · 3–4 drops to clean skin · 30ml
> For the woman who treats her skincare like a ritual — because it is one.
> *Your glow was always in there. This just helped it surface.*

### FASHION TEMPLATE
Hook: [The feeling of wearing this — not the look of the garment]
Sensory Story: [How the fabric moves. How it fits. How she feels in it.]
Functional Truth: Fabric / Fit / Colors / Sizes / Care
Identity Stamp: [The woman who wears this]
Invitation: [Soft closing line]

**EXAMPLE:**
> The dress that makes her feel like she dressed for herself — and ended up perfect for every room she walked into.
> A fluid satin midi with a wrap silhouette that adjusts to her body without asking permission. In champagne, she looks like a Sunday morning. In queen noir, she looks like a decision.
> 95% polyester satin, 5% elastane · Wrap midi, adjustable tie waist · Champagne, Blush, Queen Noir · XS–3X · Hand wash cold
> For the woman who is always dressed for something — even when nothing's planned.
> *Wear it to dinner. Wear it to wherever she's going next.*

### BEAUTY / MAKEUP TEMPLATE
Hook: [What this product gives her — confidence, presence, a feeling she reaches for]
Sensory Story: [Texture, finish, application, performance through the day]
Functional Truth: Finish / Formula / Shade range / Size / Longevity
Identity Stamp: [Her relationship with this product — through identity]
Invitation: [Soft closing line]

**EXAMPLE:**
> Her lipstick has always said things her voice didn't have to.
> A velvet finish that feels like nothing on her lips — until she looks in the mirror. Full pigment in a single swipe. Stays through the coffee, the conversation, the day, the night.
> Velvet matte · Long-wear, non-drying, cruelty-free · Blush Nude, Rose Queen, Berry Reign, Deep Plum · 3.5g · 8+ hours
> For the woman who doesn't reapply — because she didn't need to.
> *Pick one. Or don't choose at all.*

### JEWELRY TEMPLATE
Hook: [The emotional reason she buys jewelry — for herself, not for anyone else]
Sensory Story: [How it looks on. The weight. The catch of light. The layering.]
Functional Truth: Material / Plating / Dimensions / Includes / Care
Identity Stamp: [The woman who wears this — her energy, her intention]
Invitation: [Soft closing line]

**EXAMPLE:**
> She used to wait for someone to give her gold. Then she bought it for herself.
> Three delicate chains — different lengths, same luminous gold. Lightweight enough to forget. Beautiful enough that she won't.
> Brass with 18K gold plating · 16", 18", 20" · 3-piece layering set · Nickel-free, tarnish-resistant · Store in pouch, avoid water
> For the woman who is her own most thoughtful gift-giver.
> *Add all three. Because she deserves the full set.*

## LANGUAGE RULES

### Words That Elevate
Texture/Feel: silky · velvet · weightless · fluid · soft · luminous · warm · delicate · rich · smooth
Presence/Effect: lingers · settles · catches light · moves · transforms · reveals · deepens
Confidence: deliberate · intentional · effortless · signature · worthy · elevated · feminine
Sensory: warm · golden · soft · glowing · radiant · intimate · fresh · layered

### Words to Avoid
❌ "Affordable" | ❌ "Cheap" / "Budget" | ❌ "Buy now" | ❌ "Amazing" / "Incredible" / "Stunning"
❌ "High quality" (tells, doesn't show) | ❌ "Perfect for any occasion" | ❌ "You'll love it!"

---

## LENGTH GUIDELINES

| Format | Length |
|---|---|
| Short (collection page) | 1–2 sentences, hook only |
| Standard (product page) | 150–250 words |
| Extended (hero/launch) | 250–400 words |
| Email product feature | 50–100 words |
| Social caption | 2–5 sentences + CTA |

---

## THE PRODUCT DESCRIPTION TEST

Before publishing, it must pass all five:
1. Does it open with feeling, not feature? ✅ / ❌
2. Does she imagine herself with it by sentence 3? ✅ / ❌
3. Does it include real product information? ✅ / ❌
4. Does it connect to her identity — not just her shelf? ✅ / ❌
5. Does it sound like MVQUEEN, not a generic template? ✅ / ❌

If any answer is ❌ — rewrite before publishing.
