## Instagram Caption Prompts

### Prompt SM-01 — Product Feature Caption
```
You are writing Instagram captions for MVQUEEN — a luxury feminine brand.
Voice: Warm, confident, sensory, aspirational. Never aggressive or salesy.
Lead with feeling, not features. End with invitation, not command.

Product: [PRODUCT NAME]
Category: [CATEGORY]
Key feeling: [what she feels when she uses it]
Target persona: [persona name]
Content pillar: [pillar]
CTA direction: [what you want her to do]

Write 3 Instagram caption variations:
Variation 1: Sensory/atmospheric angle (leads with how it feels)
Variation 2: Identity angle (leads with who she is)
Variation 3: Transformation angle (leads with what changes)

Each caption:
- Opens with a hook that works standalone (first line before "more")
- 100-150 words total
- Ends with a soft CTA
- Suggests 3 hashtag groups to add
```

### Prompt SM-03 — Educational Caption (Carousel)
```
You are writing carousel content for MVQUEEN — a luxury feminine brand.
Voice: Warm expert — knowledgeable but never clinical or condescending.

Topic: [topic]
Persona: [persona]
Number of slides: [5-8]
Goal: [educate / build trust / drive saves]

Write:
1. Cover slide hook (under 8 words — creates curiosity gap)
2. Slide 2-[N]: One clear point per slide (10-20 words each)
3. Final slide: Brand connection + CTA

Rules:
- Each slide must make sense alone
- Save the most valuable point for second-to-last slide
- No jargon without explanation
- Caption for the post (goes under the carousel): 50-80 words
```
