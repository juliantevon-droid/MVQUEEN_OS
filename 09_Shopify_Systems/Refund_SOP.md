# 🔄 MVQUEEN — Refund & Return SOP

---

## Purpose

Handle every return and refund with warmth, efficiency, and brand integrity — turning a potentially negative experience into proof that MVQUEEN stands behind what it sells.

The goal is never to minimize refunds by making them difficult.
The goal is to resolve every issue so well that she comes back anyway.

---

## Return Policy

| Item Type | Return Window | Condition Required |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Unopened products | 30 days from delivery | Original packaging, unused |
| Opened skincare/beauty | Not eligible | Hygiene reasons — stated at purchase |
| Clothing/fashion | 30 days | Unworn, tags attached |
| Accessories | 30 days | Unused, original packaging |
| Sale items | Final sale — no returns | Clearly stated at purchase |
| Damaged/defective | Any time | Photo required |

---

## Return Process

### Customer Initiates Return
```
□ Customer contacts via email or chat
□ Acknowledge within 4 hours (business hours)
□ Confirm eligibility against return policy
□ If eligible: provide return authorization and prepaid label
□ If not eligible: explain policy warmly, offer alternative resolution
```

### Return Received
```
□ Inspect returned item on arrival
□ Confirm condition matches what was stated
□ Process refund within 2 business days of receiving item
□ Send refund confirmation email
□ Update inventory in Shopify
□ Log return reason in database
```

### Refund Processing
| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|---------------|----------------|
| Credit/debit card | 5-10 business days to appear |
| Shop Pay | 5-7 business days |
| PayPal | 3-5 business days |
| Store credit | Immediate |

---

## Refund Without Return (Exceptions)

Issue a refund or replacement WITHOUT requiring return when:
- Product arrived damaged (photo proof sufficient)
- Wrong item was sent
- Item lost in shipping and claim confirmed
- Order value under $30 (cost of return shipping exceeds value)
- Customer has strong purchase history (VIP — resolve immediately)

**Rule:** The cost of goodwill is almost always less than the cost of a lost customer.

---

## Refund Response Templates

**Eligible return:**
*"Of course — we want your experience with MVQUEEN to feel right. I'm sending your return label now. Once we receive the item, your refund will be processed within 2 business days. Thank you for giving us the chance to make this right. 🤍"*

**Ineligible return (opened beauty):**
*"We completely understand the frustration, and we're sorry the product didn't work as you hoped. Because of the nature of beauty products, we're unable to accept returns once opened — but we'd love to help you find the right product for you. Can you tell me a little more about what you were hoping for?"*

**Damaged product:**
*"We're so sorry this arrived damaged — that's not the experience we want for you at all. I'm sending a replacement out to you with priority shipping today. You don't need to return the damaged item."*

---

## Return Analytics

Track monthly:
- Total returns by product
- Return rate by category
- Top return reasons
- Return rate trend (should decrease as product quality improves)

High return rates on specific products trigger: product review, supplier audit, or listing accuracy check.
