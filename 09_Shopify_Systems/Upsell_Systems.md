# ⬆️ MVQUEEN — Upsell Systems

---

## Purpose

Increase average order value and customer lifetime value by introducing relevant, well-timed product recommendations that feel like personal suggestions — not sales tactics.

The MVQUEEN upsell philosophy:
**Recommend like a knowledgeable friend. Never push like a salesperson.**

---

## Upsell vs Cross-Sell

| Type | Definition | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Upsell | Upgrade to higher value version | Larger size, bundle, premium version |
| Cross-sell | Add complementary product | Moisturizer + SPF, dress + accessories |
| Post-purchase upsell | Offer after payment confirmed | One-click add-on at order confirmation |

---

## Upsell Placement Strategy

### Placement 1 — Product Page
**Type:** Cross-sell
**Format:** "Pairs beautifully with..." section
**Rules:**
- Maximum 3 product recommendations
- Must be genuinely complementary — not random
- Show with price
- Never interrupt the primary purchase flow

### Placement 2 — Cart Page
**Type:** Cross-sell + threshold upsell
**Format:**
- "You might also love..." — 1-2 complementary products
- Free shipping threshold bar: *"Add $X more for free shipping"*
**Rules:**
- One recommendation maximum in cart
- Threshold bar only if customer is within $20 of free shipping

### Placement 3 — Checkout Page
**Type:** Minimal — protect conversion
**Format:** None recommended — don't risk abandonment
**Rule:** Do not upsell on the checkout page. Focus only on completing the purchase.

### Placement 4 — Post-Purchase (Order Confirmation Page)
**Type:** One-click upsell
**Format:** *"Add [product] to your order before it ships — one tap, no re-entering payment."*
**Rules:**
- One product maximum
- Must be under $30 to reduce decision friction
- Complementary to what was just purchased
- Available for 15 minutes after purchase (before order is processed)

### Placement 5 — Post-Purchase Email (Day 14)
**Type:** Soft cross-sell
**Format:** *"You've had [product] for two weeks now — here's what she pairs perfectly with..."*
**Rules:**
- Never in the immediate post-purchase sequence (too soon)
- Must feel like a natural next step — not a sales email
- One product recommendation maximum

## Bundle Architecture

Bundles increase AOV while giving the customer perceived value.

**Bundle types:**
| Bundle Type | Discount | Notes |
|------------|---------|-------|
| Starter Ritual | 10% off | 2-3 entry products |
| Full Ritual | 15% off | Complete product routine |
| Gift Set | 10% off + gift packaging | Seasonal, giftable |
| Build Your Own | 10% off when buying 3+ | Flexible, high conversion |

**Bundle pricing rule:**
Never discount more than 20% — protects margin and luxury positioning.

---

## Upsell Copy Standards

Upsell language must feel like a recommendation — not a push.

**✅ Use:**
- *"Pairs beautifully with..."*
- *"Complete the ritual with..."*
- *"Women who love [product] also reach for..."*
- *"Her natural companion..."*

**❌ Never use:**
- *"You might also want to buy..."*
- *"Don't forget to add..."*
- *"Frequently bought together"* (Amazon language)
- *"Limited time — add now!"*
