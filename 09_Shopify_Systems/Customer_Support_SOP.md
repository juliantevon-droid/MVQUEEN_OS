# 💬 MVQUEEN — Customer Support SOP

---

## Purpose

Deliver customer support so warm, efficient, and brand-aligned that every interaction — even a complaint — leaves the customer feeling better about MVQUEEN than before she reached out.

Customer support is not a cost center. It is a retention tool.

## The MVQUEEN Support Framework

Every customer interaction follows this arc:

```
ACKNOWLEDGE → UNDERSTAND → SOLVE → ELEVATE → CLOSE
```

**Acknowledge:** Validate her experience before solving anything.
Never open with a policy. Open with a human response.

**Understand:** Ask one clarifying question if needed.
Never make her explain herself multiple times.

**Solve:** Provide the clearest, most generous resolution available.
When in doubt — err toward the customer.

**Elevate:** Leave her feeling better than before she reached out.
A small unexpected gesture goes a long way.

**Close:** Warm, human, open door.
Never close a ticket in a way that feels like a door slamming.

## Issue Categories & Resolution Standards

| Issue | Resolution | Timeline |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Order status question | Provide tracking + ETA | Immediate |
| Late delivery | Acknowledge + provide carrier info + escalate if 10+ days | Same day |
| Damaged product | Replacement shipped, no return required | Same day |
| Wrong item | Correct item shipped expedited, prepaid return label sent | Same day |
| Lost package | Investigation + replacement or refund | Within 48 hours |
| Return request | Process per Refund SOP | Within 4 hours |
| Product question | Answer specifically + recommend if helpful | Within 4 hours |
| Complaint (general) | Listen, acknowledge, resolve generously | Within 2 hours |
| Negative review | Respond publicly, invite to DM, resolve privately | Within 1 hour |

## Response Templates

### Order Status
*"Your order is on its way! Here's your tracking number: [number]. Based on current carrier estimates, she should arrive by [date]. Let me know if you need anything else. 🤍"*

### Damaged Item
*"Oh no — I'm so sorry this happened. That's absolutely not the experience we want for you. I'm sending a replacement out to you today with priority shipping. You don't need to return the damaged item — just enjoy the new one when it arrives."*

### Late Delivery
*"We completely understand the frustration, and we're sorry for the wait. Your tracking shows [status]. Carrier delays are unfortunately outside our control, but we're watching it closely. If it doesn't update within [X] days, reach out and we'll make it right immediately."*

### Product Question
*"Great question! [Specific, helpful answer]. If you're also interested in [related product], it pairs beautifully with [product] for [specific reason]. Happy to help with anything else!"*

### Negative Public Comment
*"Thank you for sharing this — your experience matters to us. We'd love to make this right. Please DM us so we can take care of you personally."*

---

## Escalation Protocol

Escalate to founder immediately when:
- Customer threatens chargeback or legal action
- Order value is $150+ with unresolved issue
- Customer has 3+ orders and is experiencing a problem
- Issue involves product safety concern
- Situation is appearing publicly on social media

---

## Support Quality Standards

Monthly review:
- Average response time vs. target
- Resolution rate (resolved on first contact vs. multiple)
- Customer satisfaction (follow up with: "Did we resolve everything for you?")
- Return rate patterns indicating product issues
- Recurring questions indicating content/FAQ gap
