## Skincare FAQs

**Q: What skin types are MVQUEEN products suitable for?**
A: Our products are formulated with real women's skin in mind — not a single idealized skin type. Each product page specifies which skin types it's best suited for. If you're unsure, start with our [Starter Ritual] and see how your skin responds.

**Q: Are your products fragrance-free?**
A: Our skincare products use light, clean fragrances where fragrance is included — we'll always list it clearly in the ingredient list. If you have fragrance sensitivities, check individual product pages or reach out and we'll guide you.

**Q: How long until I see results?**
A: Honest answer: it depends on the product and your skin. Hydration and texture you'll feel within days. Tone, brightness, and long-term changes typically take 4-6 weeks of consistent use. We never overpromise — but we're confident you'll feel a difference.

**Q: Can I use multiple MVQUEEN products together?**
A: Yes — our products are formulated to work together as a complete ritual. Start with the basics and build. If you have specific layering questions, our customer care team is happy to help.

## Haircare FAQs

**Q: Will your products work on my hair type?**
A: Each product specifies which hair types it's formulated for. Our goal is to serve real women's hair — not one texture or one concern. If you're between hair types, reach out and we'll make a recommendation.

**Q: How often should I use the hair mask?**
A: Once a week as a ritual — not more. Over-treating hair with conditioning masks can actually weigh it down. Weekly is the sweet spot for most hair types.

**Q: Are your hair products safe for color-treated hair?**
A: We always specify color-safe status on each product page. When in doubt, do a strand test first — and if you have questions, reach out before purchasing.

## Shipping FAQs

**Q: How long will my order take to arrive?**
A: Orders are processed within 1-3 business days. Standard shipping takes 5-8 business days after processing. Expedited options are available at checkout.

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**
A: Yes — we ship to [countries]. International orders typically take 10-21 business days. Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the customer and vary by country.

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: You'll receive a tracking number by email as soon as your order ships. You can track it directly through the carrier's website or via your Shopify order confirmation.

**Q: My tracking hasn't updated in several days — what should I do?**
A: Carrier delays can cause tracking gaps, especially for international orders. If your tracking hasn't updated in 10 business days, reach out to us and we'll investigate immediately.

## Returns & Refunds FAQs

**Q: What is your return policy?**
A: We accept returns on unopened products within 30 days of delivery. Beauty products that have been opened are not eligible for return for hygiene reasons — but if something isn't right, reach out and we'll make it right.

**Q: My product arrived damaged — what should I do?**
A: We're so sorry. Take a photo of the damage and email us — we'll send a replacement immediately. No need to return the damaged item.

**Q: How long do refunds take?**
A: Once your return is received and processed, refunds appear within 5-10 business days depending on your payment method.
