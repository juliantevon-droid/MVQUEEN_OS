# 📐 MVQUEEN — Article Structures

---

## Purpose

A library of proven article structure frameworks for different content types — so every article has a clear arc that serves both the reader and SEO.

---

## Structure 01 — The Ultimate Guide
**Best for:** Comprehensive topic coverage, pillar content, high word count targets
**Length:** 2,000-4,000 words

```
H1: The Complete Guide to [Topic]
Intro: What this guide covers, who it's for (150 words)
H2: What is [Topic]? (300 words)
H2: Why [Topic] matters (200 words)
H2: [Main subtopic 1] (400 words)
H2: [Main subtopic 2] (400 words)
H2: [Main subtopic 3] (400 words)
H2: Common mistakes / myths (300 words)
H2: How to get started (200 words)
Close: Invitation + CTA (100 words)
```

---

## Structure 02 — The Listicle
**Best for:** Educational content, easy-to-scan topics, high shareability
**Length:** 800-1,500 words

```
H1: X [Things/Ways/Signs/Reasons] [Topic]
Intro: The setup — why this list matters (100 words)
H2: [Item 1] — [brief description] (100-150 words)
H2: [Item 2] (100-150 words)
[Continue for each item]
Close: Summary insight + CTA (100 words)
```

---

## Structure 03 — The How-To
**Best for:** Tutorial content, ritual building, step-by-step guidance
**Length:** 1,000-2,000 words

```
H1: How to [Do Something] [Desired Outcome]
Intro: Why this matters + who it's for (150 words)
H2: What you'll need (100 words)
H2: Step 1 — [Name the step] (200 words)
H2: Step 2 (200 words)
H2: Step 3 (200 words)
H2: Common questions (200 words)
Close: Encouragement + CTA (100 words)
```

---

## Structure 04 — The Comparison
**Best for:** Helping her make a decision, SEO commercial intent
**Length:** 1,000-2,000 words

```
H1: [Option A] vs [Option B] — [What She Needs to Know]
Intro: Why this comparison matters (150 words)
H2: What is [Option A]? (200 words)
H2: What is [Option B]? (200 words)
H2: Key differences (300 words)
H2: Which is right for you? (200 words)
Close: Decision framework + CTA (100 words)
```

---

## Structure 05 — The Philosophy Piece
**Best for:** Brand storytelling, community building, thought leadership
**Length:** 800-1,500 words

```
H1: [Declarative statement or question]
Intro: A woman in a moment she recognizes (150 words)
H2: The tension — what's wrong with how things are (200 words)
H2: What we believe instead (200 words)
H2: What this looks like in practice (300 words)
Close: Invitation into the brand world (100 words)
```
