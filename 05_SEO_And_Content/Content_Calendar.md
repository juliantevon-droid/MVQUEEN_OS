# 📅 MVQUEEN — Content Calendar

---

## Purpose

A master planning system for all content across every platform — ensuring consistent output, strategic alignment with campaigns, and efficient production without last-minute scrambling.

---

## Calendar Architecture

```
QUARTERLY THEMES → MONTHLY CAMPAIGNS → WEEKLY CONTENT → DAILY POSTS
```

Every piece of content connects upward to a strategic theme.
Nothing is created randomly.

---

## Quarterly Theme Framework

Each quarter has one overarching emotional theme that informs all content:

| Quarter | Theme | Direction |
|---------|-------|-----------|
| Q1 (Jan-Mar) | Becoming | New rituals, identity, fresh starts |
| Q2 (Apr-Jun) | Blooming | Growth, softness, spring energy |
| Q3 (Jul-Sep) | Presence | Slow summer, grounded, intentional |
| Q4 (Oct-Dec) | Depth | Ritual deepening, warmth, legacy |

## Campaign Integration

When a campaign is active — all content serves the campaign:

**Pre-campaign (1-2 weeks before):**
- Tease content — atmosphere, no product reveal yet
- Email list warm-up
- Stories countdown or hint series

**Campaign active:**
- Daily content tied to campaign theme
- Email sequence runs
- Paid ads live
- Stories updated daily

**Post-campaign:**
- Social proof and UGC collection
- Results content ("what happened when...")
- Transition to next theme
