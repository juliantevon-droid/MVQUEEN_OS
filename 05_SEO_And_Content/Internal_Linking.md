# 🔗 MVQUEEN — Internal Linking Strategy

---

## Purpose

Build a strategic internal linking architecture across the MVQUEEN website — improving SEO authority distribution, keeping visitors engaged longer, and guiding the customer journey from content to conversion.

---

## Internal Linking Philosophy

Internal links serve two masters simultaneously:
1. **Search engines** — distribute page authority, help Google understand site structure
2. **Humans** — guide her naturally from one relevant piece of content to the next

Every internal link should feel natural to the reader — not inserted for SEO alone.

---

## Linking Architecture

### Hub and Spoke Model

MVQUEEN's website is organized into topic clusters:

```
HUB PAGE (Pillar content — broad topic)
├── Spoke 1 (Supporting article — specific subtopic)
├── Spoke 2 (Supporting article — specific subtopic)
├── Spoke 3 (Supporting article — specific subtopic)
└── Product pages (Commercial destination)
```

**Example cluster:**
Hub: *"The Complete Guide to Building a Skincare Ritual"*
Spokes: Ingredient guides, skin type articles, routine articles
Products: Linked skincare products throughout

---

## Linking Rules

### From Blog to Blog
- Every article links to 2-3 related articles
- Use descriptive anchor text (keyword-rich but natural)
- Never use "click here" or "read more" as anchor text
- Link to relevant hub pages from spoke articles

### From Blog to Product
- Link to relevant products where naturally mentioned
- Anchor text = product name or benefit phrase
- Maximum 2-3 product links per article — not every mention

### From Product to Blog
- Product pages link to relevant educational content
- "Learn more about [ingredient]" style links
- Increases time on site and builds pre-purchase trust

### From Collection to Product
- Collection pages link to individual product pages
- Cross-sell recommendations link between products

---

## Priority Pages to Link To

Build the most internal links to these pages (they need the most authority):

| Page | Priority | Why |
|------|---------|-----|
| Homepage | High | Brand entry point |
| Bestseller collection | High | Highest conversion value |
| Hero product pages | High | Primary revenue drivers |
| Brand story / About | Medium | Trust building |
| Core blog articles | Medium | SEO authority cluster |

---

## Internal Linking Audit

**Monthly:** Check that new articles include internal links
**Quarterly:** Full site audit — identify orphaned pages (no internal links pointing to them)
**Tool:** Screaming Frog or Ahrefs site audit for comprehensive audit
