# 🎯 MVQUEEN — TikTok SEO

---

## Purpose

Optimize MVQUEEN's TikTok content for discovery — ensuring videos reach the right audience through strategic keyword use, sound selection, and content optimization.

TikTok's search function is growing rapidly. Women now search TikTok the way they search Google for beauty and lifestyle content.

---

## TikTok SEO Fundamentals

### How TikTok Discovers Content
1. **For You Page algorithm** — content signals (watch time, shares, saves, comments)
2. **TikTok Search** — growing rapidly, especially for beauty and lifestyle
3. **Hashtags** — secondary discovery tool
4. **Sounds** — trending sounds amplify reach

### What TikTok Indexes
| Element | SEO Importance |
|---------|--------------|
| Spoken words in video | High — TikTok transcribes audio |
| On-screen text | High — TikTok reads text overlays |
| Caption text | Medium |
| Hashtags | Medium |
| Video description | Medium |
| Account name | Low |

---

## Content Optimization for Search

### Speak Your Keywords
TikTok transcribes audio. Say your keywords in the video.

Instead of showing a product silently — say:
*"This luxury face serum is the one product I won't stop using"*

Instead of generic lifestyle content — say:
*"Here's how I built a morning ritual that actually makes me want to wake up"*

### Text Overlay Keywords
Put key terms as text on screen — TikTok reads them.

Every video should have at minimum:
- Hook text (first 2 seconds)
- 1-2 keyword text overlays mid-video
- CTA text at end

### Caption Strategy
Caption = TikTok's description field.

**Formula:**
```
[Hook line — first sentence must create curiosity]
[1-2 lines of context]
[3-5 hashtags]
```

## TikTok-Specific Content Rules

**Watch time is everything:**
- Hook in first 2 seconds — if she doesn't stay, nothing else matters
- No slow intros — start in the middle of the action
- Loop content where possible (end flows into beginning)
- Optimal length: 15-30 seconds for viral potential, 60-90 seconds for educational

**Sound strategy:**
- Use trending sounds when brand-appropriate
- Original audio builds brand identity over time
- ASMR/soft sounds perform well for MVQUEEN aesthetic
- Always check sound is not copyrighted before posting
