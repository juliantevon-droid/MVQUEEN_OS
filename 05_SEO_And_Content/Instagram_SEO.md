## Profile SEO Optimization

### Username
`@mvqueen` or `@mvqueenbrand` — brand name searchable, memorable

### Name Field (not username — the bold display name)
**Format:** Brand Name + Primary Keyword
**Example:** `MVQUEEN | Luxury Feminine Beauty`
**Alternative:** `MVQUEEN | Skincare & Rituals`

**Rule:** The name field is indexed by Instagram search. Use it as a keyword field.

### Bio Optimization
**Formula:**
```
Line 1: What MVQUEEN is (keyword-rich, 1 sentence)
Line 2: Who it's for (persona language)
Line 3: What she gets (value proposition)
Line 4: CTA + link
```

**Example:**
```
Luxury feminine beauty & rituals ✨
For women who choose softness intentionally
Skincare • Hair • Fashion • Accessories
👇 Shop your ritual
[link]
```

## Content SEO Optimization

### Caption Strategy
- First 125 characters are most indexed — lead with keywords naturally
- Write for humans first, search second
- Include 1-2 primary keywords per caption where natural
- Never keyword-stuff — Instagram's algorithm detects unnatural patterns

### Manual Alt Text (Critical — Most Brands Skip This)
Instagram allows manual alt text on every image.
Write descriptive, keyword-aware alt text for every post.

**How to add:** Post → Advanced Settings → Accessibility → Write alt text

**Formula:** `[What's in the image] + [product/brand keyword] + [mood/atmosphere]`
**Example:** `Luxury skincare serum on marble surface, MVQUEEN morning ritual, soft light`

### Keyword Categories to Target
| Category | Example Keywords |
|----------|----------------|
| Brand | luxury feminine beauty, MVQUEEN skincare |
| Product | luxury face serum, feminine skincare routine |
| Lifestyle | soft life aesthetic, intentional living, morning ritual |
| Identity | feminine lifestyle, softness aesthetic, elevated living |

## Instagram Search Optimization

Instagram users search for:
- Brands by name → Optimize username and name field
- Topics → Optimize captions and hashtags
- Aesthetics → Optimize visual consistency (algorithm learns your aesthetic)
- Products → Optimize product tags and captions

**Trending search behavior to capitalize on:**
- "luxury skincare routine"
- "soft life aesthetic"
- "feminine morning routine"
- "clean beauty routine"
- "luxury self care"
