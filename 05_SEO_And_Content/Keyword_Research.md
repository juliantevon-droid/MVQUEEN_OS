## PART 8 — PLATFORM-SPECIFIC KEYWORD STRATEGY

### Google Search
**Target:** Transactional and informational intent
**Priority keywords:** Primary + Secondary from all categories
**Match types:** Exact + Phrase for branded; Broad modified for category

### Pinterest
**Target:** Inspirational and planning intent
**Keyword format:** Natural phrases women actually search, not keyword strings
**Priority:** Lifestyle, fashion, skincare — high save categories
**Key insight:** Pinterest search volume is separate from Google — terms like "soft life aesthetic" perform significantly higher on Pinterest

### TikTok Search
**Target:** Discovery and tutorial intent
**Priority keywords:** "How to," "tutorial," product category + aesthetic
**Key insight:** TikTok search is conversational — optimize for phrases, not single keywords

### Instagram
**Target:** Discovery via hashtags (declining relevance) + bio keyword optimization
**Priority:** Brand identity keywords in bio; hashtags support Reels discovery
