# 👑 MVQUEEN HOOK SYSTEMS
*The complete system for opening lines that stop her scroll and pull her in*

---

## PURPOSE

A hook has one job: make her stop. Not pause — stop.

When a MVQUEEN hook lands correctly, her internal response is: *she's talking about me.*

---

## THE 6 PSYCHOLOGICAL TRIGGERS

1. **Identity Recognition** — She sees herself. "That's me."
2. **Emotional Desire** — She feels what she wants to feel.
3. **Pattern Interrupt** — She reads something she didn't expect.
4. **Curiosity** — She has to know what comes next.
5. **Aspiration** — She sees who she's becoming.
6. **Validation** — She feels understood and affirmed.

The strongest hooks combine two or more triggers in a single sentence.

## HOOK WRITING RULES

### Every hook must
- Be written for one specific woman — not a general audience
- Activate at least one of the six psychological triggers
- Feel like MVQUEEN — warm, confident, feminine
- Make her want to read the next sentence

### Hooks never
- Open with the product name or product feature
- Use exclamation marks to create false energy
- Use generic openers ("Are you tired of...?", "Introducing...", "We're excited to...")
- Talk at her from above

---

## THE HOOK TEST

1. Does it make her feel seen? ✅ / ❌
2. Does she want to read the next sentence? ✅ / ❌
3. Does it sound like MVQUEEN? ✅ / ❌
4. Could it work for any generic brand? If yes ❌ — rewrite.
5. Is it specific enough to feel personal? ✅ / ❌

A hook that passes all five is ready. Any failure = back to the desk.
