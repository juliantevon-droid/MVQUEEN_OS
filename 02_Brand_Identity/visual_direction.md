# 7. Visual Composition Rules

### **1. Soft Edges**
Avoid harsh lines or aggressive geometry.

### **2. Warm Whites Only**
No pure white backgrounds.

### **3. Minimal Clutter**
Luxury = intentional simplicity.

### **4. Feminine Curves**
Use rounded shapes, soft silhouettes, gentle arcs.

### **5. Emotional Centering**
The subject should feel emotionally present, not distant.

### **6. Warm Shadows**
Avoid cold or blue shadows.

### **7. Balanced Negative Space**
Whitespace is part of the luxury identity.

- cluttered compositions  
- aggressive shadows  
- pure white backgrounds  
- pure black backgrounds  
- overly sharp edges

- corporate‑style visuals
